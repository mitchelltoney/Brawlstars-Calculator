Best counters to Max

  • Crow

    Slows Max's speed-based pressure with Slowing Toxin, while poison interrupts regeneration and keeps her vulnerable between engagements.

  • Gus

    Outranges Max's four-projectile bursts, then out-sustains her chip damage through healing spirits and Spooky's shield.

  • Pam

    Out-tanks Max's chip damage with steady turret healing, while Scrapsucker drains the ammo sustaining her pressure.

  • Stu

    Outmaneuvers Max's four-projectile bursts with repeated Super dashes, while Gaso-Heal sustains him through prolonged pressure.

Also weak to: Tank Assassin Spawner
