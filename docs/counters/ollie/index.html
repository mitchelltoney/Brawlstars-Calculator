Best counters to Ollie

  • Colette

    Shreds Ollie's high health with percentage-based shots, then interrupts his Super dash by carrying and stunning him.

  • Otis

    Outranges Ollie's low-damage soundwaves and silences him before he can begin a Super dive or follow-up attack.

  • Surge

    Repels Ollie's dives with Super knockback, then upgrades his range and burst to punish renewed approaches.

  • Lou

    Controls Ollie's approach with freeze buildup, reducing his damage through Hypothermia and interrupting his Super dash with stuns.

  • RT

    Bursts Ollie at close range by splitting instantly, where overlapping head and leg attacks punish his forced approach.
