Leon Counters

Best picks against Leon in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Leon

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Leon at ambush range, while Counter Crush returns part of every burst he lands.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Leon up close, while Tough Guy reduces incoming burst damage and favors an extended duel.

  • Shelly

    Punishes Leon's close-range ambush with shotgun burst, then Shell Shock slows him before he can disengage.

  • Crow

    Tracks Leon through invisibility with repeated poison ticks, while Extra Toxic weakens his close-range ambush.

  • Chester

    Bursts Leon down at his preferred close range, where multiple bells converge before he can retreat invisibly.

  • Charlie

    Stops Leon's ambush with Cocoon as he emerges, isolating him before his close-range burst can finish.

  • Emz

    Controls Leon's approach with broad spray damage, then Friendzoner pushes him beyond his blades' maximum-damage range.

  • Mortis

    Outmaneuvers Leon's blade volleys with dashes, then Combo Spinner adds the burst needed to finish the close duel.

Leon is a strong pick against

  • Bo

    Approaches Bo invisibly past his ranged control, then uses Invisiheal to sustain point-blank pressure.
