Best picks against Juju in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Juju

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Juju and Gris-Gris, absorbing their pressure until heavy hammer swings remove the doll and let him advance.

  • Draco

    Absorbs Juju's artillery pressure, then Dragon Solo's speed and damage reduction let him close the gap and overwhelm her.

  • El Primo

    Out-tanks Juju's artillery and leaps over her wall cover, turning her range advantage into a close-range punch fight.

  • Tick

    Outranges Juju on open ground with lingering mines, controlling lanes beyond her standard artillery reach.

  • Larry

    Outranges Juju's ground throws and adds Lawrie's pressure, forcing her to spend attacks clearing a second threat.

Also weak to: Wallbreaker

Juju is a strong pick against

  • Charlie

    Bombards Charlie's Spiders from behind cover and forces her yo-yo to divide attention with Gris-Gris.

  • Emz

    Threatens Emz from beyond walls as Gris-Gris enters her vulnerable angle and hinders escape.
