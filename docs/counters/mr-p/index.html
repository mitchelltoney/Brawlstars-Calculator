Mr P Counters

Best picks against Mr P in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Mr P

  • Gene

    Out-sustains Mr P's porter pressure by continuously healing nearby teammates, denying the chip damage his spawnables need to accumulate.

  • Max

    Outpaces Mr P's suitcase pressure and reloads faster while moving, maintaining fire on porters without becoming an easy target.

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Mr P's suitcase and porter damage, then gains damage reduction after rolling directly onto him.

  • Tick

    Bombards Mr P and his porter base from artillery range, using faster reloads to sustain lane pressure.

  • Edgar

    Bypasses Mr P's porter screen with Lets Fly, landing behind him and overwhelming his weak close-range defense.

  • Mortis

    Dashes past Mr P's porter screen and suitcase bounces, forcing the controller into an unfavorable close-range fight.

  • Bibi

    Clears Mr P's porters with wide, piercing bat swings, then Home Run's speed closes distance before replacements accumulate.

Mr P is a strong pick against

  • Bea

    Keeps Bea occupied with recurring porters, draining her lone ammo without charging Big Sting and breaking her lane pressure.

  • Gene

    Sends recurring porters into Gene's single-target shots and pull, exploiting his slow reload to overwhelm his lane.

  • Glowy

    Overloads Glowy's single-target glowbeam with respawning porters, then pressures him beyond its range using bouncing suitcases.

  • Gray

    Feeds porters into Gray's non-piercing shots to drain his ammo while bouncing suitcases pressure him behind cover.

  • Grom

    Pressures Grom behind cover with bouncing suitcases, driving him from walls before he establishes artillery control.

  • Gus

    Keeps Gus firing at recurring porters while suitcase explosions maintain pressure around cover and exhaust his ammo.
