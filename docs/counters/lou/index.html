Lou Counters

Best picks against Lou in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Lou

  • Stu

    Outmaneuvers Lou's narrow three-cone volleys with frequent Super dashes, escaping his ice field before freeze can accumulate.

  • Belle

    Outranges Lou with precise bolts, denying him enough hits to build freeze.

  • Penny

    Blocks Lou's non-piercing volleys with Salty Barrel, then pressures him behind cover using long-ranged cannon fire.

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Lou's narrow volleys, then pressures him at close range before freeze accumulates.

  • Kenji

    Evades Lou's linear volleys with frequent dashes, then sustains close-range pressure through damage-based healing.

  • Edgar

    Reaches Lou with Lets Fly, then bursts and heals at point-blank range before repeated volleys can build freeze.

  • Lily

    Bursts Lou at close range after Vanish bypasses his firing lane, denying him time to build freeze.

  • Nita

    Overloads Lou's non-piercing volleys with her bear, while Bear With Me sustains it whenever she hits him.

Also weak to: Thrower

Lou is a strong pick against

  • Byron

    Freezes Byron with rapid volleys, disrupting his attack rhythm before damage-over-time darts establish sustained pressure.

  • Draco

    Repeatedly freezes Draco to disrupt sustained pressure while Hypothermia steadily reduces his damage.

  • Meeple

    Outranges Meeple and stacks freeze until a stun lands while Hypothermia reduces their return damage.

  • Meg

    Repeatedly freezes Meg's slow Mecha, interrupting its long barrage while Hypothermia reduces its return damage.

  • Ollie

    Builds freeze during Ollie's approach, using Hypothermia to reduce his damage before stuns interrupt his Super dash.

  • Otis

    Builds freeze on Otis, using Hypothermia to reduce his damage before stuns interrupt his ink volleys.

  • Pam

    Freezes Pam inside her stationary healing zone, where slippery terrain disrupts each slow Scrapstorm unload.

  • Pearl

    Stacks freeze on Pearl while Hypothermia suppresses her heated damage until a full freeze interrupts the next volley.

  • Ruffs

    Destabilizes Ruffs with accumulating freeze and slippery terrain, separating him from stationary sandbag cover.

  • Sam

    Freezes and stuns Sam during his rushes while Hypothermia weakens his close-range punches.

  • Sandy

    Freezes Sandy with repeated volleys, interrupting his low-damage attacks and denying sustained control inside the Sandstorm.

  • Shade

    Builds freeze on Shade's short-range approach, interrupting its pressure before center hits can connect.

  • Sirius

    Controls Sirius and clustered Shadows inside a freezing zone, where Supercool accelerates buildup across the group.
