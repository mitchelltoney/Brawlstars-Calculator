Best counters to Lou

  • Stu

    Outmaneuvers Lou's narrow three-cone volleys with frequent Super dashes, escaping his ice field before freeze can accumulate.

  • Belle

    Outranges Lou with consistent bolts, gaining shields on hits while denying him enough contact to complete a freeze.

  • Penny

    Blocks Lou's non-piercing volleys with Salty Barrel, then pressures him behind cover using long-ranged cannon fire.

  • Mortis

    Bursts Lou at close range after Creature Of The Night bypasses his volleys while immune, preventing freeze buildup.

  • Kenji

    Outmaneuvers Lou's linear volleys with alternating dashes, then heals from each close-range slash while sustaining pressure.

  • Edgar

    Bursts Lou at point-blank range after Let's Fly lands behind him, healing through stray cones before freeze builds.

  • Lily

    Bursts Lou at close range after Vanish bypasses his firing lane, denying him time to build freeze.

  • Nita

    Overloads Lou's non-piercing volleys with her high-health bear, then heals it whenever her piercing shockwaves hit him.
