Tara Counters

Best picks against Tara in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Tara

  • Bibi

    Out-tanks Tara's slow-reloading card volleys, then rushes through her range and clears clustered weak Shadows with wide swings.

  • Darryl

    Rolls past Tara's long-range card poke, then uses tank health and point-blank burst to punish her slow reload.

  • Otis

    Outranges Tara's spread cards, then prevents her from attacking or using Gravity before bursting her down.

  • Sandy

    Pierces through Tara and her grouped Shadows, then out-sustains her slow reload with Healing Winds inside the Sandstorm.

  • Janet

    Slightly outranges Tara's spread cards, pierces through summoned Shadows, and escapes Gravity by launching into the air.

Also weak to: Assassin Thrower

Tara is a strong pick against

  • Clancy

    Pressures Clancy's early form with three attacking Shadows, draining his ammo before concentrated card damage closes the exchange.

  • Gale

    Pulls Gale from his preferred range, then unloads close-range card damage before knockback restores his defensive spacing.

  • Gene

    Floods Gene with three Shadows that absorb his slow shots and intercept his pull, exhausting his single-target pressure.

  • Surge

    Outranges Surge before his range upgrade, then deploys three attacking Shadows that exploit his slow reload without charging his Super.
