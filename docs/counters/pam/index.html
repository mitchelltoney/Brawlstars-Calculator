Pam Counters

Best picks against Pam in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Pam

  • Lou

    Controls Pam's stationary healing zone with freezes and slippery terrain, repeatedly interrupting her slow Scrapstorm unload.

  • 8bit

    Outranges Pam's inaccurate long-distance Scrapstorm and amplifies his damage with Damage Booster, overpowering her turret's gradual healing.

  • Buster

    Blocks Pam's slow projectile stream with Montage, turning her sustained fire against her while closing into stronger Lens Flare range.

  • Jessie

    Punishes Pam's turret-centered positioning with bouncing shots that strike her and the healing station from one projectile.

  • Colette

    Punishes Pam's high health with percentage-based shots, converting every heal into more damage on the next hit.

  • Lumi

    Pierces through Pam and her healing turret on both throw and recall, punishing the stationary positioning her sustain requires.

Pam is a strong pick against

  • Amber

    Breaks Amber's continuous flame pressure with Scrapsucker, draining her ammunition and reclaiming enough to keep firing.

  • Mandy

    Uses high health and turret healing to withstand Mandy's isolated shots, demanding several accurate hits before going down.

  • Max

    Sustains through Max's chip pressure with turret healing while Scrapsucker strips the ammo behind her offense.
