Shelly Counters

Best picks against Shelly in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Shelly

  • Squeak

    Outranges Shelly's shotgun with delayed explosions, while Residue slows her approach before she can close for burst damage.

  • Carl

    Outranges Shelly with outbound and returning pickaxe hits, keeping her where the shotgun's spread lands fewer pellets.

  • Piper

    Outranges Shelly's shotgun, while Auto Aimer knocks her back if she reaches close range.

  • Stu

    Kites Shelly's shotgun with repeated Super dashes, while Gaso-Heal offsets chip damage and denies clean close-range bursts.

  • Nita

    Out-sustains Shelly behind the bear, as Bear With Me heals Nita and restores her summon during sustained pressure.

  • Spike

    Outranges Shelly with split cactus spikes, punishing her limited reach before her shotgun can deliver concentrated damage.

  • Penny

    Outranges Shelly with coin volleys, while Salty Barrel blocks incoming shells and triggers punishing gold-pouch splash.

Also weak to: Thrower

Shelly is a strong pick against

  • Bibi

    Overwhelms Bibi up close with shotgun damage and knocks her away before her delayed bat swing lands.

  • Edgar

    Overwhelms Edgar with concentrated point-blank pellets before his repeated punches can recover enough health.

  • El Primo

    Punishes El Primo's need to stay close, shredding his health before Super knockback pushes him beyond punching range.

  • Fang

    Bursts Fang at point-blank range and interrupts his follow-up kicks with Super knockback.

  • Kaze

    Overpowers Kaze at close range, then knocks her back before she can chain another strike.

  • Leon

    Punishes Leon's close-range ambush with shotgun burst, then Shell Shock slows his escape.

  • Lily

    Punishes every Lily teleport with point-blank shotgun damage and Super knockback before she can continue attacking.

  • Moe

    Punishes Moe's Driller approach with shotgun pressure, then uses Shell Shock to slow his immediate retreat.

  • Mortis

    Bursts Mortis at shotgun range, then Shell Shock slows his retreat before he can dash away.

  • Nori

    Bursts Nori at close range, then uses Shell Shock to stop him from disengaging.

  • Rico

    Reaches Rico with Fast Forward, reloads completely, then overwhelms his low health with concentrated shotgun damage.

  • Rosa

    Overpowers Rosa up close with shotgun bursts and repeated Super knockbacks that break her punching rhythm.
