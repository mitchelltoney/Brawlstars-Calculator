Colette Counters

Best picks against Colette in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Colette

  • Stu

    Dodges Colette's linear shots and return path with chainable dashes, while Gaso-Heal restores the chip she lands.

  • Bea

    Outranges Colette, while Honeycomb provides a shield against her percentage combo and a supercharged shot supplies heavy fixed burst.

  • Griff

    Out-sustains Colette's shrinking percentage shots with Business Resilience, while close-range coin bursts punish her charge's predictable return.

  • Jessie

    Overloads Colette's single-target pressure with Scrappy, while Energize repairs the turret and forces her to spend more ammo.

  • Crow

    Kites Colette with fast movement and Slowing Toxin, limiting her healing while avoiding both passes of her Super.

  • Charlie

    Absorbs Colette's non-piercing shots with Spiders, while Cocoon cancels her charge before its return hit.

  • Ruffs

    Uses Take Cover to block Colette's non-piercing shots, forcing her to spend ammo before damaging him.

  • Meeple

    Pressures Colette through cover, then uses Ragequit to interrupt her charge before its return hit.

Also weak to: Sniper Thrower

Colette is a strong pick against

  • Bolt

    Uses percentage-based shots to punish Bolt's huge health pool before he builds enough speed for dangerous collisions.

  • Bonnie

    Turns Bonnie's durable cannon form against her, using percentage-based shots to deal heavier opening damage.

  • Damian

    Turns Damian's durability against him because every shot scales with his remaining health instead of treating it as protection.

  • Dynamike

    Uses projectile speed and reach to keep Dynamike moving in exposed lanes, reducing the trading value of his slow-fusing throws.

  • Fang

    Softens Fang during his approach with health-scaled fire, saving Push It to reset distance when he commits.

  • Frank

    Punishes Frank's massive health with scaling fire, while Push It can disrupt his Super and line up both charge passes.

  • Meeple

    Breaks Meeple's wall-centered positioning with Push It, then maintains pressure from beyond their effective range using health-scaled fire.

  • Meg

    Makes Meg's high-health Mecha a liability by dealing percentage-based damage that erases its usual durability advantage.

  • Nita

    Removes Nita's main pressure source from beyond her reach because her summoned bear receives reliable fixed damage.

  • Ollie

    Stops Ollie's Super dash by carrying him to maximum range and stunning him after percentage-based shots punish his health.

  • Otis

    Pairs Gotcha healing with current-health shots, allowing sustained midrange exchanges against Otis without losing the war of attrition.

  • Pam

    Turns Pam's high health and healing against her, since each percentage-based shot scales from her restored health.
