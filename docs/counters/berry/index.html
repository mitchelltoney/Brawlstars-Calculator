Best counters to Berry

  • Barley

    Outranges Berry and stacks overlapping puddle damage, while Berry's damage-over-time zones cannot stack against the same target.

  • Kenji

    Dashes through Berry's puddle zones, healing on every hit while punishing his low health and slow reload.

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Berry's lingering puddles and into close range, punishing his low health and long reload between thrown cones.
