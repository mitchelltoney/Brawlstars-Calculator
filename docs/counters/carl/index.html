Carl Counters

Best picks against Carl in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Carl

  • Buzz

    Bursts Carl down at close range and stuns him out of his spinning Super, denying its sustained damage.

  • Surge

    Bursts Carl at close range and knocks him out of his spinning Super, turning his dive into a losing trade.

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Carl up close, then Barrel Roll interrupts his spinning Super and sets up point-blank double-shot burst.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Carl at point-blank range and pulls him out of his spinning Super, exposing him to circular area damage.

  • Gene

    Stops Carl's spinning dive with Lamp Blowout, pushing him away and canceling the Super before it deals sustained damage.

Carl is a strong pick against

  • Amber

    Uses Protective Pirouette to withstand Amber's sustained fire while maintaining pressure through her flames.

  • Jacky

    Keeps Jacky outside her short attack radius, landing outward and returning pickaxe hits before she closes the distance.

  • Janet

    Out-trades Janet at equal range by landing both passes of each pickaxe against her single focused shot.

  • Ruffs

    Pierces Ruffs's sandbags with each pickaxe throw, negating his cover while threatening hits on both passes.

  • Shelly

    Keeps Shelly beyond effective shotgun range, where her spread lands fewer pellets while the pickaxe hits twice.

  • Spike

    Outlasts Spike's chip damage and punishes his low health with pickaxe hits on both travel paths.
