Best counters to Fang

  • Colette

    Outranges Fang’s kick with percentage-based shots, then Push It carries him away whenever he commits to a dive.

  • El Primo

    Out-tanks Fang at melee range, absorbing his combo before faster-reloading punches overwhelm his slower kick sequence.

  • Griff

    Punishes Fang’s committed dive with dense coin volleys, concentrating their damage at point-blank range for heavy burst.

  • Chester

    Bursts Fang after his dive with escalating bell volleys, concentrating every projectile at point-blank range before Fang can disengage.

  • Clancy

    Punishes Fang’s dive with a wide Super volley, turning every landed paintball into progress toward stronger stages.

  • Otis

    Silences Fang as he dives, preventing attacks and Supers while sustained ink volleys finish him.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Fang at point-blank range, with Super knockback interrupting his follow-up before he can chain close-range kicks.

  • Gale

    Denies Fang’s dive with displacement, as Twister stops his Super and knockback keeps him outside kick range.
