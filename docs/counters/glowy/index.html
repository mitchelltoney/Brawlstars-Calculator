Best counters to Glowy

  • Eve

    Outranges Glowy's beam, while hatchlings overwhelm his limited single-target damage and pressure him with poison.

  • Barley

    Attacks safely over walls, breaking Glowy's tether line of sight while stacked puddles outdamage his low-burst beam.

  • Mr P

    Overloads Glowy's single-target glowbeam with respawning porters, while bouncing suitcases pressure him beyond its range.

  • Gene

    Outranges his glowbeam with wide chip, then pulls Glowy away from teammates to break his support tether.

  • Bonnie

    Outranges and out-tanks his glowbeam in cannon form, then launches onto him with high close-range burst.
