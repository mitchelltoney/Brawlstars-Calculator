Best counters to Byron

  • Clancy

    Farms upgrade tokens from Byron's repeatedly healed frontline, then overwhelms his gradual sustain with an upgraded projectile barrage.

  • Lou

    Rapid volleys repeatedly freeze Byron, breaking his attack rhythm before his damage-over-time darts establish sustained pressure.

  • Sprout

    Bombards Byron from behind cover, while Hedge walls block the straight firing angles his narrow darts require.

  • Piper

    Bursts down Byron's low health at maximum range before his damage-over-time darts or self-healing can swing the duel.

Also weak to: Sniper

Byron is a strong pick against

    Outranges Crow and stacks damage over time beyond his kunai reach, forcing retreats before his poison pressure develops.
