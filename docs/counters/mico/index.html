Mico Counters

Best picks against Mico in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Mico

  • Penny

    Deploys her cannon beside Mico's landing point, where Master Blaster deals damage and knocks him beyond follow-up range.

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Mico's jump burst and uses Tar Barrel to slow each landing before unloading both shotgun waves.

  • RT

    Punishes every landing with overlapping split-form blasts, bursting Mico during the grounded window before he can jump again.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Mico's limited jump burst and punishes every landing with concentrated point-blank shotgun damage during his slow reload.

  • Stu

    Kites his jumps with chained Nitro Boost dashes, then fires during each landing window before repositioning again.

Also weak to: Thrower

Mico is a strong pick against

  • Barley

    Leaps across walls and Barley's slow bottle arcs, reaching melee distance to exploit his low health.

  • Dynamike

    Crosses Dynamike's delayed blasts in mid-jump and lands close enough to eliminate him before a counterattack develops.

  • Grom

    Bypasses Grom's delayed explosives by jumping, lands beside him, and overwhelms his unreliable point-blank defense.

  • Sprout

    Crosses Sprout's Seed Bombs and Hedge barriers by jumping, then drains ammo to suppress the thrower's response.

  • Tick

    Clears Tick's mine pattern during an invulnerable jump, landing beside him to overwhelm his low health with rapid melee attacks.
