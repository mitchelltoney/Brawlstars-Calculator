Janet Counters

Best picks against Janet in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Janet

  • Belle

    Outranges Janet's fully focused shots and reloads faster, winning repeated long-range trades before she can retaliate.

  • Squeak

    Controls Janet's firing lanes with delayed explosions, while Residue slows her repositioning between focused shots.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Janet's low sustained damage, advancing through focused shots before heavy swings force her backward.

  • Carl

    Out-trades Janet at equal range because his returning pickaxe can hit twice, delivering more pressure than her focused single shot.

  • Kenji

    Closes through Janet's focusing window with his base dash, then heals on hits while close-range slashes overwhelm her.

Also weak to: Thrower

Janet is a strong pick against

  • Chester

    Limits Chester's bell spread to glancing damage from afar and crosses walls with Backstage Pass if he approaches.

  • Emz

    Pokes Emz beyond her strongest aerosol overlap, using aerial movement to leave the slowing Super below.

  • Eve

    Threads focused soundwaves through Eve and her hatchlings, removing the swarm without surrendering offensive momentum.

  • Frank

    Pressures Frank beyond hammer reach, with jetpack flight denying both his windup attack and stunning Super.

  • Gale

    Lands concentrated damage on Gale from maximum distance, where his spreading snowballs connect only sparsely.

  • Sam

    Keeps Sam chasing beyond punching distance and vaults across walls with Backstage Pass when he reaches her.

  • Sandy

    Builds focused damage on Sandy before his shorter-ranged pebble bursts can threaten retaliation.

  • Tara

    Maintains a range edge over Tara, clears lined-up Shadows, and lifts away from Gravity's pull.
