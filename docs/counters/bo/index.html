Bo Counters

Best picks against Bo in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Bo

  • Stu

    Dodges Bo's sweeping arrows and delayed mines with repeatable Super dashes, denying the control his kit depends on.

  • Max

    Outmaneuvers Bo's arrows and mine zones with superior speed, maintaining pressure as movement accelerates her reload.

  • Leon

    Bypasses Bo's ranged control while invisible, then sustains the close approach with continuous healing during his Super.

  • Piper

    Outranges Bo's arrows, landing maximum-damage shots before he can contest her lane or establish threatening minefields.

Also weak to: Sniper

Bo is a strong pick against

  • Lola

    Catches Lola and her Ego in the same exploding arrows while mines restrict the lanes supporting their sustained fire.

  • Pearl

    Controls Pearl's advance with hidden mines, using Snare A Bear to stun her before she unloads heated cookies.

  • Penny

    Breaks the walls protecting Penny and her cannon with mines, exposing both to sustained arrow pressure.

  • Poco

    Outranges Poco's low-damage waves and turns hidden mines into stuns with Snare A Bear, creating focus-fire openings.

  • RT

    Controls RT's approach with mines, then uses Snare A Bear to stun his short-ranged split form.
