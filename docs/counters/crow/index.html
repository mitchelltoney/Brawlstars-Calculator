Crow Counters

Best picks against Crow in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Crow

  • Spike

    Curveball makes cactus spikes harder to dodge, punishing Crow's evasive movement and low health with reliable burst.

  • Rosa

    Out-tanks Crow's poison chip and shields through his close-range dagger burst, surviving long enough to land repeated punches.

  • Gus

    Outranges Crow and shields through poison chip, winning repeated balloon trades before Crow's Super can force a close-range burst.

  • Byron

    Outranges Crow and layers damage over time beyond his kunai reach, forcing retreats before Crow's poison pressure builds.

  • Piper

    Outranges Crow and bursts through his low health, then knocks him back whenever he closes.

  • Charlie

    Overloads Crow's non-piercing daggers with Spiders, forcing repeated ammo expenditure before he can pressure Charlie directly.

Also weak to: Sniper Tank

Crow is a strong pick against

  • Amber

    Weakens Amber's continuous flame pressure with Extra Toxic, winning extended trades whenever poison remains active.

  • Colette

    Restricts Colette's movement with Slowing Toxin, creating room to avoid her round-trip Super while poison reduces her recovery.

  • Larry

    Evades Larry's delayed blasts, maintaining poison to suppress his retreat healing and punish each attempt to attack from cover.

  • Leon

    Keeps tabs on invisible Leon through poison damage, and Extra Toxic reduces the danger of an ensuing melee ambush.

  • Mandy

    Slips past Mandy's narrow projectiles with quick movement, while lingering poison prevents recovery before the next ranged duel.

  • Max

    Neutralizes Max's movement advantage with Slowing Toxin and keeps poison ticking to delay regeneration between engagements.

  • Mortis

    Stays beyond Mortis's dash reach, then uses Slowing Toxin to disrupt attack chains and hinder his escape.

  • Starr Nova

    Keeps Starr Nova under ranged poison, limiting her healing and using Extra Toxic to soften close-quarters damage.
