Best counters to Ziggy

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Ziggy's delayed strike circles and closes before his slow teleport escape can activate.

  • Edgar

    Jumps directly onto Ziggy, then rapid healing punches overwhelm his low health before delayed lightning can land nearby.

  • Kaze

    Outmaneuvers Ziggy's delayed strikes with fast movement and dashes, then bursts his low health at close range.

  • Kenji

    Dashes out of Ziggy's delayed strike circles, then heals from each hit while pressuring his low health at close range.

  • Kit

    Punishes Ziggy's delayed lightning by approaching invisibly, then pounces to stun him before his teleport can activate.
