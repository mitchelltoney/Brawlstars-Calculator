Mandy Counters

Best picks against Mandy in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Mandy

  • Crow

    Outmaneuvers her narrow shots with fast movement, while poison denies Mandy recovery between long-range exchanges.

  • Gus

    Out-sustains her single-shot poke with spirit healing, while the Super shield absorbs Mandy's burst damage.

  • Stu

    Closes on her through repeated Nitro Boost dashes, dodging narrow candies before bursting Mandy at close range.

  • Pam

    Out-tanks her single-shot damage with high health and turret healing, forcing Mandy to land repeated shots for an elimination.

  • Nani

    Punishes Mandy's telegraphed Super with Return To Sender, reflecting much of its damage back into her.

  • Tick

    Denies her stationary Focus with lobbed mines over walls, repeatedly forcing Mandy to abandon long-range firing lanes.

  • Mortis

    Closes past her range with chained dashes, exploiting Mandy's low health and lack of knockback at close range.

Also weak to: Thrower

Mandy is a strong pick against

  • Bolt

    Outranges Bolt while Focused, then Caramelize slows his speed buildup whenever crowd-control immunity is inactive.

  • Brock

    Outranges Brock while Focused, landing faster candies before his slower rockets can threaten the firing position.

  • Rico

    Outranges Rico while Focused, then Cookie Crumbs pierces his wall cover before bouncing shots create pressure.
