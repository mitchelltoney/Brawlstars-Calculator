Starr Nova Counters

Best picks against Starr Nova in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Starr Nova

  • Damian

    Out-tanks her short-range burst and punishes her up close before sword-form healing can overcome his large health pool.

  • Crow

    Poisons her from range, reducing incoming healing and weakening her short-range burst with Extra Toxic.

  • Otis

    Silences her at close range, preventing sword-form attacks and self-healing while extra ink drops finish the burst.

  • Piper

    Outranges her short-range hair burst and uses Auto Aimer's knockback to deny sword-form pressure when she closes in.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks her short-range burst with massive health, then punishes her commitment before sword-form healing can swing the duel.

  • Buster

    Out-tanks her short-range burst and absorbs her commitment, leaving sword-form healing unable to overcome his sustained close-range pressure.

  • Ash

    Out-tanks her short-range burst, builds Rage through the exchange, and overwhelms her before sword-form healing catches up.
