Gale Counters

Best picks against Gale in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Gale

  • Tara

    Pulls Gale out of defensive spacing, then bursts him down at close range before knockback can reset the lane.

  • Penny

    Outranges Gale’s snowballs, while her cannon pressures him over walls and forces constant repositioning.

  • Charlie

    Outranges Gale’s snowballs, then Cocoon temporarily sidelines his knockback during decisive team fights.

  • Belle

    Outranges Gale by nearly two tiles, poking safely beyond his snowballs while her mark amplifies every follow-up hit.

  • Janet

    Deals full damage at maximum range, while Gale’s wide volley only connects with a few snowballs.

  • Amber

    Wins ranged trades with continuous fire, while Gale’s wide spread lands only partial damage in return.

Also weak to: Spawner Thrower

Gale is a strong pick against

  • Alli

    Slows Alli's entry with Freezing Snow and blows her back before she can chain consecutive dashes.

  • Bolt

    Breaks Bolt's acceleration cycle through slows and displacement before his crowd-control immunity comes online.

  • Bull

    Maintains distance from Bull with slows and knockback, preventing him from delivering his strongest point-blank damage.

  • Buzz

    Pressures Buzz from range and meets Torpedo Throw with broad knockback, interrupting the dive before his close-range combo begins.

  • Chester

    Slows Chester with Freezing Snow and preserves the distance that blunts his multi-bell burst.

  • Damian

    Pressures Damian from beyond punch range and resets his advance with knockback before sustained contact develops.

  • El Primo

    Layers slows and knockback against El Primo, keeping him from reaching the close range his punch sequence demands.

  • Fang

    Places Twister in Fang's Super path, halting the dive before knockback maintains separation from his kicks.

  • Gus

    Uses repeated slows to dictate distance from Gus, shoving him away whenever he approaches his healing spirits.

  • Hank

    Slows and pushes Hank beyond Balloon Blast reach, forcing him to rebuild ideal spacing for every charge.

  • Jacky

    Slows Jacky throughout her approach and resets the spacing with knockback whenever she enters attack range.

  • Moe

    Repels Moe's Driller entry, with Blustery Blow stunning him whenever the knockback drives him against an obstacle.

  • Mortis

    Throws Mortis out of each dive, with Blustery Blow stunning him against terrain before he can dash again.

  • Nori

    Knocks Nori away during his dive, then chains slowing attacks to shut down every re-entry.
