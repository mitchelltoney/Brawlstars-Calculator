Best counters to Meg

  • Clancy

    Punishes Meg's durable Mecha by farming upgrade tokens, then overwhelms both forms with escalating multi-projectile burst.

  • Cordelius

    Isolates Meg in the Shadow Realm, disabling her remount while rapid double shots strip both health bars.

  • Buzz

    Closes through Meg's ranged Mecha pressure with a stun, then unloads five close-range hits before she can disengage.

  • Colette

    Shreds Meg's high-health Mecha with percentage-based attacks, preventing its durability from creating favorable damage trades.

  • Lou

    Repeatedly freezes Meg's slow Mecha, interrupting its long barrage while Hypothermia reduces its return damage.

  • Lola

    Outranges Meg's Mecha and doubles long-range pressure with her Ego, winning sustained trades before Meg closes.

  • Bea

    Outranges Meg's Mecha and cycles Supercharged shots into its high health, breaking the suit before its swing reaches.
