Best counters to Stu

  • Spike

    Blocks Stu's non-piercing fireworks with Life Plant, denying the hit he needs to keep chaining Nitro Boosts.

  • Ruffs

    Blocks Stu's non-piercing fireworks with sandbags, denying the one-hit Super charge his dash-heavy game plan depends upon.

  • Penny

    Blocks Stu's non-piercing fireworks with Salty Barrel, denying the hit he needs to recharge Nitro Boost immediately.

  • Sandy

    Catches Stu's lateral dashes with wide, piercing sand attacks, then hides inside Sandstorm to deny reliable Super recharges.

  • Poco

    Out-sustains Stu's repeated poke with healing, while broad piercing attacks catch him throughout his lateral dashes.

  • Belle

    Outranges Stu's fireworks and pressures his moderate health from afar, forcing risky approaches before he can begin chaining dashes.
