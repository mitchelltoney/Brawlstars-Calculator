Meeple Counters

Best picks against Meeple in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Meeple

  • Colette

    Outranges Meeple with percentage-based shots, while Push It displaces them and disrupts their wall-based lane control.

  • Lou

    Outranges Meeple and stacks freeze from safety, eventually stunning them while Hypothermia cuts their return damage.

  • Lola

    Outranges Meeple, while her Ego absorbs single-projectile pressure and adds a second stream of damage from another angle.

  • Bea

    Outranges Meeple and converts opening hits into Supercharged follow-ups, punishing them before their wall-piercing zone controls cover.

  • Ruffs

    Outranges Meeple with bouncing lasers, while Take Cover sandbags absorb their single homing shots and deny clean lanes.

  • Charlie

    Overloads Meeple's single projectile with seeking spiders, forcing wasted ammo and denying clean homing shots.

  • Lily

    Bypasses Meeple's range with Vanish, reappearing at point-blank range to burst before homing shots control her approach.

  • Squeak

    Punishes Meeple's reliance on cover because Sticky Blomb explosions reach through walls, while Residue slows their escape.

  • Spike

    Pressures Meeple around cover with split spikes, while Life Plant absorbs their single-projectile shots.

Meeple is a strong pick against

  • Chester

    Guides homing pawns into Chester from beyond his reach, reserving Ragequit to stun and repel any close approach.

  • Colette

    Fires through Colette's cover and saves Ragequit to cancel her charge before the second pass connects.

  • Griff

    Sends slightly homing shots toward Griff, then denies his close-range advantage by stunning and pushing him away before the burst lands.

  • Mina

    Pins Mina outside combo range with long homing shots, using Ragequit to halt any dash that closes the gap.
