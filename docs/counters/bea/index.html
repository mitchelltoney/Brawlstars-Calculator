Bea Counters

Best picks against Bea in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Bea

  • Mr P

    Forces Bea to spend her lone ammo on porters that do not charge Big Sting, repeatedly interrupting her lane pressure.

  • Piper

    Bursts Bea down at equal range with front-loaded damage before her lone ammo cycles a Supercharged reply.

  • Sprout

    Lobs Seed Bombs over cover and raises hedges against Bea's straight shots, controlling lanes she cannot contest.

Also weak to: Thrower Sniper

Bea is a strong pick against

  • Colette

    Outranges Colette and uses Honeycomb to blunt her percentage-based combo before punishing her with fixed Supercharged burst.

  • Gray

    Outranges Gray's narrow Finger Pistol, landing wider shots and Supercharged follow-ups to control the long-range exchange.

  • Jae-Yong

    Outranges Jae-Yong, landing high-damage shots before he closes enough distance for his piercing attacks to connect.

  • Lola

    Outranges Lola and cycles normal and Supercharged shots to disrupt her firing lane before the Ego establishes crossfire.

  • Meeple

    Outranges Meeple and converts opening hits into Supercharged follow-ups before their wall-piercing zone can control cover.

  • Meg

    Outranges Meg's Mecha and chains Supercharged shots through its high health, breaking the suit before its swing can connect.
