Penny Counters

Best picks against Penny in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Penny

  • Nani

    Outranges Penny’s gold pouches and bursts her down before the cannon can establish sustained map pressure.

  • Kit

    Bypasses Penny’s range with a leap, stunning her for teammates before her slow reload can answer.

  • Squeak

    Zones Penny and her cannon together with wide delayed explosions, denying the static setup her artillery pressure requires.

  • Kenji

    Exploits Penny’s slow reload with alternating dashes and slashes, healing through her pouch damage while closing into melee.

  • Bo

    Breaks the walls sheltering Penny and her cannon with mines, exposing both to sustained long-range arrow pressure.

Also weak to: Thrower

Penny is a strong pick against

  • 8bit

    Bombards 8bit's stationary Damage Booster from artillery positions, using Salty Barrel to absorb his return fire.

  • Charlie

    Turns Charlie's Spiders into gold-pouch splash targets while cannon fire reaches her beyond the yo-yo's range.

  • Clancy

    Slows Clancy's token growth as Salty Barrel absorbs paintballs and cannon shots reach him over cover.

  • Eve

    Bursts gold pouches through Eve's hatchling swarm, clearing the summons while the splash reaches her.

  • Frank

    Outranges Frank with coin volleys, then uses Master Blaster to knock him back and interrupt his windup.

  • Gale

    Outranges Gale's snowballs and shells him over walls, forcing him to abandon protected positions.

  • Gene

    Uses Salty Barrel to intercept Gene's projectiles and pull, preventing his team from isolating a target.

  • Lily

    Catches Lily's teleport projectile on Salty Barrel, leaving her short-range advance exposed to a gold-pouch burst.

  • Lola

    Splashes gold pouches through Lola's nearby Ego, making her stacked positioning expose both targets at once.

  • Lou

    Uses Salty Barrel to absorb Lou's volleys, then bombards his position from the other side of cover.

  • Maisie

    Shelters behind Salty Barrel from Maisie's pressure rockets while cannon blasts dislodge her from protected angles.

  • Mico

    Places the cannon at Mico's landing spot so Master Blaster damages him and knocks him out of follow-up range.

  • Moe

    Uses Salty Barrel as cover against Moe's bouncing shots and as the trigger for punishing pouch splash.

  • Shelly

    Keeps Shelly beyond shotgun range while Salty Barrel absorbs shells and provides a target for gold-pouch splash.

  • Sirius

    Turns Sirius's clustered Shadows into splash targets, bursting gold pouches through them to damage him.

  • Stu

    Places Salty Barrel between Stu and each firework, preventing the hit that would immediately restore Nitro Boost.
