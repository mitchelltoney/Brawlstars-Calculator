Pierce Counters

Best picks against Pierce in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Pierce

  • Glowy

    Out-sustains Pierce with Parasitism, while fear disables his attacks and interrupts his shell-collection rhythm.

  • Najia

    Outranges Pierce with redirected snake shots, while stacking poison keeps damaging him throughout his slow reload cycles.

  • Ziggy

    Punishes Pierce’s shell routes with delayed area strikes, while the wide storm forces him away from ammo pickups.

  • Jae-Yong

    Evades Pierce’s narrow shots with frequent speed boosts, while switching into Party Mode heals back his weaker opening damage.

  • Sirius

    Overloads Pierce’s single-target shots with summoned Shadows that drop no shells, starving his reload while they pressure him.

Pierce is a strong pick against

  • Gigi

    Fires beyond Gigi's spin range, then knocks her away after she teleports in and forces another approach.
