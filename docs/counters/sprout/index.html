Sprout Counters

Best picks against Sprout in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Sprout

  • Mico

    Leaps over Sprout's Seed Bombs and Hedge walls, then steals ammo to limit the thrower's return fire.

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Sprout's Seed Bombs and closes to melee range before the artillery thrower can restore spacing.

  • Edgar

    Jumps behind Sprout to bypass the Hedge and artillery range, then heals through Seed Bomb damage with rapid punches.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Sprout's Seed Bomb damage, then uses a long-range pull to bring the thrower within striking distance.

  • Darryl

    Rolls across Sprout's artillery range and survives Seed Bombs with post-roll damage reduction before unloading at point-blank range.

Also weak to: Tank Assassin

Sprout is a strong pick against

  • Bea

    Lobs Seed Bombs over cover and raises Hedge walls, denying Bea the straight lanes her shots require.

  • Byron

    Bombards Byron from behind cover while Hedge walls obstruct the straight firing angles his narrow darts require.

  • Rosa

    Outranges Rosa's punches with arcing Seed Bombs, then blocks her approach with Hedge walls she cannot quickly bypass.

  • Ruffs

    Bombards Ruffs over walls, exploiting his low health and driving him away from the sandbags anchoring his position.
