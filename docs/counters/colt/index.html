Best counters to Colt

  • Piper

    Wins long-range peek trades against Colt because one bullet delivers its damage at once while his volley requires sustained tracking.

  • Belle

    Out-trades Colt at maximum range because Positive Feedback shields her against the remaining bullets in each volley.

  • Gus

    Out-sustains Colt's long volleys with higher health and a Super shield strong enough to absorb an entire attack.

Also weak to: Sniper

Colt is a strong pick against

  • Chuck

    Capitalizes on Chuck's early Post setup, sending long-range six-bullet strings into his Heist safe before his route comes online.
