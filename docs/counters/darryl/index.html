Best picks against Darryl in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Darryl

  • Cordelius

    Traps Darryl in the Shadow Realm without Barrel Roll access, using boosted speed and reload to kite his shotguns.

  • Otis

    Outranges Darryl's shotguns, then silences him after Barrel Roll before he can unleash point-blank burst.

  • Clancy

    Farms upgrade tokens from Darryl's tank health, then upgraded multi-projectile attacks punish his predictable straight-line roll-ins.

Darryl is a strong pick against

    Passes straight through Buster's Montage barrier during the roll, creating an opening for two waves of point-blank shells.
