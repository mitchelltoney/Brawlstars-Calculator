Nita Counters

Best picks against Nita in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Nita

  • Stu

    Outranges Nita's shockwaves and kites her bear with repeated Nitro Boost dashes, preventing either from sustaining contact.

  • Mortis

    Outmaneuvers Nita's shockwaves and her bear with attack dashes, slipping past both to pressure her directly.

  • Colette

    Outranges Nita and burns down her bear with fixed spawnable damage, denying her strongest source of pressure.

  • Poco

    Out-sustains Nita's pressure with repeated healing and pierces her bear to keep damaging Nita behind it.

  • Kenji

    Outmaneuvers Nita's bear with alternating dash-and-slash attacks, healing on hits to sustain pressure after closing the gap.

  • Lily

    Bypasses Nita's bear through the Shadow Realm, then reappears beside her for rapid close-range attacks.

Also weak to: Thrower

Nita is a strong pick against

  • Bull

    Outlasts Bull in close fights by cycling healing between herself and her bear whenever either lands damage.

  • Cordelius

    Outranges Cordelius with wide shockwaves while her bear absorbs paired mushrooms and drains his limited ammo.

  • Draco

    Blocks Draco's non-piercing dragon flames with her bear while Bear With Me sustains both partners.

  • Lou

    Overloads Lou's non-piercing volleys with her bear while Bear With Me keeps the summon healthy.

  • Piper

    Overloads Piper's slow single-target reload as her bear absorbs shots and disrupts open sniper lanes.

  • Sam

    Outnumbers Sam with her bear at close range, then stuns him to punish committed melee exchanges.

  • Sandy

    Outlasts Sandy's modest damage by cycling Bear With Me healing whenever she or her bear lands a hit.

  • Shelly

    Outlasts Shelly behind her bear as Bear With Me alternately restores each partner during sustained pressure.
