Best counters to Chuck

  • Cordelius

    Stops Chuck's Post dashes by sending him to the Shadow Realm, removing his Super route and access to objectives.

  • Clancy

    Bursts Chuck at predictable Post endpoints, turning defensive hits into tokens before upgraded volleys overwhelm his high health.

  • Colt

    Outdamages Chuck in Heist base races with long-range six-bullet volleys while he spends time placing Posts.

  • Bull

    Outdamages Chuck in Heist base races with point-blank shotgun bursts and survives his predictable passes with tank-level health.

  • Brock

    Outdamages Chuck's setup in Heist with long-range rockets, pressuring the safe before his Post network reaches full value.

  • Melodie

    Outdamages Chuck in Heist base races, chaining dashes to the safe while orbiting notes deliver sustained damage.

  • Angelo

    Outranges Chuck with charged shots at fixed Post endpoints, then uses Stinging Flight to evade arriving dashes.
