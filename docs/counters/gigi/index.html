Best counters to Gigi

  • Finx

    Outranges her spin, then rewinds to his earlier position with restored ammo when she commits her delayed teleport.

  • Pierce

    Outranges her spin, then knocks her back when she teleports in, resetting the distance she needs to deal damage.

  • Alli

    Uses stealth to approach, then a health-scaled dash bursts Gigi before her spinning damage ramps up.

  • Trunk

    Out-tanks her spinning damage and controls their shared close range with a wider attack circle and ant-boosted damage.

  • Sirius

    Outranges her spin with a lobbed projectile, then crowds her with controllable Shadows before she can reach Sirius.
