RT Counters

Best picks against RT in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to RT

  • Brock

    Destroys cover with Rocket Fuel, exposing RT's stationary split legs to long-range splash damage.

  • Piper

    Matches RT's maximum range while Auto Aimer pushes his split form away before its short-range damage connects.

  • Stu

    Dodges RT's thin projectiles with repeated Super dashes, while Gaso-Heal restores health through any chip damage that lands.

  • Gus

    Out-sustains RT's ranged chip with spirit healing, and Health Bonanza strengthens each recovery between marked follow-up shots.

  • Max

    Dodges RT's thin shots with superior speed, while Phase Shifter grants immunity against marked follow-up damage during the dash.

  • Bo

    Controls RT's approach with mines, while Snare A Bear stuns his short-ranged split form before it closes the gap.

Also weak to: Thrower

RT is a strong pick against

  • Alli

    Meets Alli's short-range dash with overlapping blasts from both split-form halves, turning her required approach into a burst window.

  • Bonnie

    Controls Bonnie's cannon form from beyond its range, then marks her so every later hit deals more damage.

  • Kenji

    Keeps Kenji outside striking distance with marks that strengthen later shots before his short dash closes the gap.

  • Kit

    Turns Kit's forced point-blank engagement against him, landing blasts from both halves in split form.

  • Lily

    Pressures Lily from range, then activates Out Of Line to answer her dive with an immediate close-range blast.

  • Melodie

    Surrounds himself with split-form burst that catches Melodie whenever her dive brings the orbiting notes into range.

  • Mico

    Times overlapping split-form blasts for Mico's landings, dealing burst damage while he is grounded between jumps.

  • Ollie

    Prepares split form with Out Of Line, then catches Ollie's dash between simultaneous head and leg blasts.
