Best picks against RT in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to RT

  • Brock

    Lands wide rocket explosions on RT's large hitbox, then Rocket Fuel removes cover protecting his stationary split legs.

  • Piper

    Matches RT's maximum range while Auto Aimer pushes his split form away before its short-range damage connects.

  • Stu

    Dodges RT's thin projectiles with repeated Super dashes, while Gaso-Heal restores health through any chip damage that lands.

  • Gus

    Out-sustains RT's ranged chip with spirit healing, and Health Bonanza strengthens each recovery between marked follow-up shots.

  • Max

    Dodges RT's thin shots with superior speed, while Phase Shifter's immunity nullifies a marked follow-up hit during a dash.

  • Bo

    Controls RT's normal-speed movement with mines, and Snare A Bear's stun interrupts his split-form pressure before it connects.
