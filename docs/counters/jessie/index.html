Jessie Counters

Best picks against Jessie in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Jessie

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Jessie's pressure and pierces through Scrappy with broad hammer swings, damaging both before she can repair the turret.

  • Belle

    Outranges Jessie and bounces Electro-Bolts between her and Scrappy, turning the turret into another source of pressure.

  • Squeak

    Overwhelms Scrappy with delayed area explosions, denying Jessie the stationary turret control that anchors her lane.

Also weak to: Thrower

Jessie is a strong pick against

  • Colette

    Diverts Colette's single-target fire into Scrappy, then restores the turret with Energize to prolong her ammo burden.

  • Otis

    Sustains Scrappy with Energize so Otis must repeatedly spend non-piercing volleys before contesting her equal range.

  • Pam

    Chains one projectile across Pam and her healing station, turning their shared position into simultaneous damage.

  • Pearl

    Interposes Scrappy against Pearl's heated volleys, draining the resource she needs for a stronger burst.

  • Piper

    Taxes Piper's slow reload by adding Scrappy, while Energize restores the turret between her sniper rounds.

  • Poco

    Spreads bouncing damage across Poco's clustered team while Scrappy adds another threat his group healing must cover.
