Surge Counters

Best picks against Surge in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Surge

  • Spike

    Outranges Surge before his range upgrade, while Life Plant absorbs shots that would otherwise charge his Super.

  • Ruffs

    Outranges Surge's early stages, while Take Cover blocks narrow shots that would otherwise charge his next upgrade.

  • Tara

    Outranges Surge before his range upgrade, then three attacking Shadows overwhelm his slow reload without charging his Super.

  • Charlie

    Outranges Surge before his range upgrade and wastes his slow reload on three spiders that do not charge his Super.

Also weak to: Thrower Spawner

Surge is a strong pick against

  • Ash

    Uses upgraded speed and reach against Ash, reserving Super knockback for his Rage-driven close-range advances.

  • Bibi

    Keeps Bibi outside bat range and answers each delayed swing or advance with a jumping Super that dodges and repels her.

  • Buzz

    Interrupts Buzz's grapple with an invulnerable Super jump, knocking him away before his close-range burst starts.

  • Carl

    Meets Carl's spinning dive with close-range burst and Super knockback, turning his committed attack against him.

  • Cordelius

    Finishes Cordelius in three shots during Shadow Realm duels, ending the fight before his reload advantage develops.

  • Edgar

    Readies Super knockback for Edgar's landing, interrupting his healing punches before following with ranged shots.

  • Ollie

    Checks Ollie's dives with Super knockback, then leverages later speed and range upgrades against repeat attempts.

  • Ruffs

    Uses upgraded mobility to catch Ruffs and Super jumps over sandbag cover to exploit his low health.

  • Sam

    Knocks Sam out of close-range dives with Super, then relies on upgraded speed and reach to preserve separation.
