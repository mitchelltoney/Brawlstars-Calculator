Best counters to Emz

  • Janet

    Outranges her maximum-damage spray zone with focused notes, while jetpack flight escapes her slowing Super when pressured.

  • Mortis

    Dashes inside her spray's close-range blind spot, where each cloud damages him only once before he bursts her down.

  • Barley

    Bombards her over walls beyond hairspray retaliation, forcing movement through lingering puddles while healing from every attack.

  • Juju

    Controls Emz over walls with arcing elemental shots, while Gris-Gris pressures her blind spot and slows retreat.

  • Larry

    Lobs twice-exploding attacks over cover beyond her spray, while Lawrie pressures the close-range blind spot.
