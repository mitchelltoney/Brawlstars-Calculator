Lily Counters

Best picks against Lily in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Lily

  • Belle

    Outranges Lily, then Grounded prevents reloading after a mark, limiting the burst available following her teleport.

  • Penny

    Blocks Lily's teleport projectile with Salty Barrel, then punishes her short-range approach with gold-pouch splash.

  • Amber

    Maintains sustained fire outside Lily's attack range, then Dancing Flames punishes her forced point-blank teleport.

  • RT

    Outranges Lily until she dives, then Out Of Line enables an immediate split-form blast at point-blank range.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Lily and attacks in every direction, so teleporting behind her never escapes point-blank damage.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Lily's burst and wins point-blank trades with shotgun damage, forcing her to disengage before securing a takedown.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Lily down at point-blank range, where her shotgun and knockback Super punish every teleport engagement.

Lily is a strong pick against

  • Lou

    Uses Vanish to cross Lou's firing lane and burst him before he can build freeze.

  • Meeple

    Uses Vanish to cross Meeple's range, reappearing point-blank before homing shots can control her approach.

  • Nita

    Uses Vanish to evade Nita's bear, then reappears beside her for rapid close-range attacks.
