Chester Counters

Best picks against Chester in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Chester

  • Janet

    Kites at maximum range so only one spread bell connects, then uses Backstage Pass when Chester closes in.

  • Amber

    Sustains long-range fire through Chester's slow reload windows, denying the close spacing where multiple spread bells connect.

  • Gale

    Freezing Snow slows Chester on hit, keeping him outside the range needed for his multi-bell burst.

  • Stu

    Outmaneuvers Chester with repeated Super dashes, dodging spread volleys and punishing his slow reload between attacks.

  • Ruffs

    Uses Take Cover to intercept Chester's bells, reducing his burst and forcing extra reloads.

  • Meeple

    Outranges Chester with slightly homing pawns, then uses Ragequit to stun and knock him back when he closes.

  • Emz

    Punishes Chester's approach with stacking hairspray damage, then uses Friendzoner to push him back into the lingering cloud.

  • Charlie

    Uses Spiders to soak Chester's bells, reducing his multi-projectile burst while the summons pressure him at close range.

Chester is a strong pick against

  • Alli

    Punishes Alli's short-range dash by concentrating multi-bell volleys before she can disengage.

  • Damian

    Concentrates later multi-bell volleys into Damian at close range, bursting through his tank health.

  • Dynamike

    Punishes Dynamike at close range with clustered bells that land before his delayed explosives detonate.

  • Edgar

    Punishes Edgar at point-blank range by concentrating multiple bells where his short punches must connect.

  • Fang

    Punishes Fang after his dive with concentrated bell volleys before he can disengage.

  • Frank

    Punishes Frank's stationary windups with close-range multi-bell volleys, connecting every bell during his attack.

  • Griff

    Overwhelms Griff's moderate health by stacking later bell volleys faster than his extended coin attack finishes.

  • Kaze

    Catches Kaze during close-range dives with multi-bell volleys, denying time to chain strikes or retreat.

  • Kenji

    Overwhelms Kenji's attack-based healing at close range by concentrating multiple bells into each volley.

  • Kit

    Concentrates multi-bell damage on Kit during close-range commitments, denying him time to secure another attachment.

  • Leon

    Concentrates multiple bells into Leon at close range before invisibility can cover his retreat.

  • Mortis

    Overloads Mortis with sequenced bells while he commits at close range, cutting off time for another dash.

  • Nori

    Combines close-range bell burst against Nori with random stuns or slows that disrupt his escape.

  • Otis

    Overwhelms Otis at close range with converging bells before his ink attack finishes unloading.
