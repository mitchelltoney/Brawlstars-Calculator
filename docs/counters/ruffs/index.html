Ruffs Counters

Best picks against Ruffs in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Ruffs

  • Surge

    Outpaces Ruffs after upgrading, then uses Super jumps to breach sandbag cover and burst his low health.

  • Lou

    Locks Ruffs down with accumulating freeze, while his Super's slippery zone drives him away from stationary sandbag cover.

  • Griff

    Bursts down low-health Ruffs at midrange, while Piggy Bank removes walls that amplify his bouncing laser pressure.

  • Cordelius

    Isolates low-health Ruffs from teammates in the Shadow Realm, where close-range mushroom burst overwhelms him without support.

  • Buzz

    Crosses Ruffs's long range with Torpedo Throw, stunning and bursting him after landing inside his sandbag cover.

  • Carl

    Pierces Ruffs's sandbags with each boomerang throw, invalidating his cover and threatening two hits per attack.

  • Sprout

    Bombards Ruffs over walls, exploiting his low health and forcing him away from the sandbags anchoring his position.

Also weak to: Assassin

Ruffs is a strong pick against

  • Chester

    Places Take Cover in Chester's firing lane, making his bells hit sandbags and draining the reloads behind his burst.

  • Colette

    Forces Colette's non-piercing shots into Take Cover, consuming her ammo before she can damage him directly.

  • Griff

    Fires beyond Griff's coin range while sandbags intercept the volley, denying the clean approach his close-range burst needs.

  • Meeple

    Bounces lasers into Meeple from beyond their range while Take Cover catches homing shots before they find a clean lane.

  • Otis

    Lets Take Cover absorb Otis's shots and Super, then maintains safe pressure by bouncing lasers around walls.

  • Piper

    Makes Piper spend limited sniper ammo breaking Take Cover instead of landing her high-damage rounds on him.

  • Stu

    Feeds Stu's fireworks into sandbags, denying the hit that charges his Super and sustains his dash cycle.

  • Surge

    Keeps early-stage Surge outside firing range and blocks narrow shots with Take Cover, slowing his path to the next upgrade.
