Best counters to Tick

  • Mico

    Leaps over Tick's delayed mines while invulnerable, lands at melee range, and periodically steals ammo from his slow reload.

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Tick's delayed mines, closes on his low health, and unloads point-blank before the mines detonate.

  • Darryl

    Rolls across Tick's minefield, then uses post-roll damage reduction to survive nearby blasts while unloading point-blank shells.

  • Bibi

    Crosses Tick's delayed zoning with tank health and increased speed, then batters his low health at close range.

  • Kenji

    Dashes through gaps in Tick's delayed minefield, then heals through close-range attacks while overwhelming his low health.
