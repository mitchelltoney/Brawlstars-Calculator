Best picks against Jacky in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Jacky

  • Carl

    Outranges Jacky's short Groundbreaker radius, repeatedly landing returning pickaxe hits before she can reach him.

  • Kenji

    Out-sustains Jacky's close-range pressure by instantly healing recent damage, then uses his base dash to disengage between exchanges.

  • Belle

    Outranges Jacky's short attack radius and slows her approach with a trap, denying the close contact she needs.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Jacky with superior health, while Active Noise Canceling prevents her Super from pulling him into repeated strikes.

  • Gale

    Keeps Jacky outside her attack radius by slowing each approach, then pushes her away whenever she finally closes.

  • Rosa

    Out-tanks Jacky at close range, absorbing Groundbreaker with her Super's damage reduction before winning the sustained trade.

Also weak to: Tank
