Best counters to Kit

  • Tick

    Outranges Kit's claws with persistent mines, while Last Hurrah's shield absorbs and repels his close-range dive.

  • Edgar

    Out-sustains Kit at close range, using Fisticuffs-enhanced punch healing to survive and punish his committed dive.

  • Kenji

    Out-sustains Kit in close quarters, restoring his burst with Hosomaki Healing after the initial commitment.

  • Charlie

    Separates Kit from an attached teammate by Cocooning his host, removing his healing support and exposing both targets.

  • Cordelius

    Sends Kit's host into the Shadow Realm, forcibly detaching him and stripping away his healing support.

  • RT

    Bursts Kit down at point-blank range in split form, where both halves punish his forced close engagement.

  • Chester

    Bursts Kit down at his preferred close range, where multiple bells converge before he can attach again.
