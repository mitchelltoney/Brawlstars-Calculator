Best counters to Kit

  • Tick

    Outranges Kit with persistent mines, while Last Hurrah absorbs damage and punishes his committed close-range dive.

  • Edgar

    Out-sustains Kit at close range, using Fisticuffs to amplify punch healing and punish his committed dive.

  • Kenji

    Out-sustains Kit in close quarters, using Hosomaki Healing to recover from his initial burst.

  • Charlie

    Cocoon interrupts Kit's close-range commitment, isolating him before he can capitalize on the dive.

  • Cordelius

    Isolates Kit in the Shadow Realm, cutting him off from teammates and denying coordinated support.

  • RT

    Bursts Kit down at point-blank range in split form, where both halves punish his forced close engagement.

  • Chester

    Bursts Kit down at his preferred close range, where multiple bells converge before he can attach again.
