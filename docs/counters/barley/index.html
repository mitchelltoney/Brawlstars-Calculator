Barley Counters

Best picks against Barley in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Barley

  • Mico

    Leaps over walls and slow bottles, bypassing Barley's cover to punish his low health at close range.

  • Kenji

    Dashes around Barley's slow projectiles and heals with each hit, overwhelming his low health at close range.

  • Edgar

    Jumps directly onto Barley, then unloads rapid healing punches faster than his low-health target can escape or reload.

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Barley's slow bottles and into close range, exploiting his low health before he can reload.

Also weak to: Assassin

Barley is a strong pick against

  • Berry

    Outranges Berry and layers overlapping puddles, winning damage-over-time exchanges because Berry's zones cannot stack on one target.

  • Charlie

    Pressures Charlie over walls and uses lingering puddles to clear Spiders before they can absorb incoming attacks.

  • Emz

    Bombards Emz over walls beyond hairspray range, forcing movement through lingering puddles while Medical Use heals each throw.

  • Glowy

    Bombards Glowy over walls, breaking tether sightlines while overlapping puddles outdamage his low-burst beam.

  • Lumi

    Lobs lingering puddles over walls around Lumi, denying space while cover blocks her forward-thrown morning stars.

  • Maisie

    Pressures Maisie over walls and blankets approaches with lingering puddles, denying clean lines for her slow-starting rockets.

  • Najia

    Outlasts Najia's poison with Medical Use, healing on every artillery throw while pressuring her over walls.

  • Willow

    Outlasts Willow's delayed poison with Medical Use, restoring health on every throw throughout their artillery exchanges.
