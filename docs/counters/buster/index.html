Best picks against Buster in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Buster

  • Rosa

    Out-tanks Buster up close with her damage-reduction Super and faster reload, surviving Lens Flare while maintaining punch pressure.

  • Darryl

    Out-bursts Buster at point-blank range after rolling inside Montage, bypassing its barrier and overwhelming his slower reload.

  • Jacky

    Bypasses Montage with circular Groundbreaker damage, while Counter Crush returns Buster's strongest close-range hits.

  • Buzz

    Out-DPSes Buster at point-blank range with five-hit attacks and a one-second reload, while Torpedo Throw supplies the approach and stun.

  • El Primo

    Out-tanks Buster in sustained melee, using faster-reloading punches and Flying Elbow Drop to erase his range advantage.
