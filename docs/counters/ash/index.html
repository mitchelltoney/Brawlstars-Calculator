Ash Counters

Best picks against Ash in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Ash

  • Rosa

    Out-tanks Ash in close quarters by shielding through his Rage damage, then punishing him with boosted Super punches.

  • Frank

    Outlasts Ash in direct tank duels because Sponge grants extra health against his Rage-driven pressure.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Ash because Tough Guy reduces damage at low health, blunting the pressure generated by Ash's Rage.

  • Surge

    Kites Ash after upgrading and knocks him back with Super jumps whenever Rage-fueled pressure reaches close range.

  • Lumi

    Kites Ash as Half-Time slows him with recalled morning stars, denying his Rage-fueled close-range pressure.

Also weak to: Tank Anti-Tank

Ash is a strong pick against

  • Squeak

    Absorbs Squeak's delayed Sticky Blombs to build Rage, gaining the speed and damage needed to close distance.

  • Starr Nova

    Outlasts Starr Nova's short-range burst, builds Rage through the exchange, and overwhelms her before sword-form healing catches up.
