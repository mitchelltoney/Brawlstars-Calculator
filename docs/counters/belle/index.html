Belle Counters

Best picks against Belle in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Belle

  • Nani

    Outranges Belle after the orbs converge and threatens a three-projectile burst, forcing her from open sniper lanes.

  • Darryl

    Tanks Belle's poke long enough to roll into point-blank range and overwhelm her with shotgun burst.

  • Piper

    Bursts Belle down at equal range with heavier maximum-distance shots before her faster reload can matter.

Belle is a strong pick against

  • 8bit

    Stops 8bit from sustaining boosted fire by using Grounded to block his reloads after a mark.

  • Amber

    Attacks Amber beyond her reach, then Grounded interrupts the reload cycle she needs for sustained flame pressure.

  • Clancy

    Pressures Clancy beyond his reach and reloads faster, denying the repeated hits that advance his stages.

  • Colt

    Out-trades Colt at maximum range as Positive Feedback absorbs damage from later bullets in each volley.

  • Damian

    Kites Damian beyond punching range, landing Electro-Bolts before he can close the distance.

  • Gale

    Pokes Gale from beyond his snowballs, then marks him to amplify every follow-up hit.

  • Gene

    Outranges Gene's full-damage projectile and punishes healing clusters by bouncing Electro-Bolts between nearby allies.

  • Jacky

    Slows Jacky's approach with a trap while firing from beyond her short attack radius.

  • Janet

    Outranges Janet's fully focused shots and reloads faster, winning repeated lane trades before she retaliates.

  • Jessie

    Punishes Jessie for placing Scrappy nearby, chaining Electro-Bolts across both targets from superior range.

  • Lily

    Keeps Lily at range, then uses Grounded to restrict her post-teleport burst by preventing reloads.

  • Lola

    Punishes Lola for stacking beside her Ego, chaining Electro-Bolts through both targets from outside her range.

  • Lou

    Maintains range against Lou, denying him repeated hits and preventing his freeze buildup.

  • Maisie

    Outranges Maisie and uses Positive Feedback to absorb damage during open-lane trades before Shockwave threatens.

  • Najia

    Breaks Najia's single-ammo cycle with Grounded, blocking the reload needed for another snake or extended Super.

  • Stu

    Pressures Stu beyond his fireworks' reach, forcing risky approaches before he can start chaining dashes.
