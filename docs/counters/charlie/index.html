Charlie Counters

Best picks against Charlie in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Charlie

  • Penny

    Punishes Spiders with gold-pouch splash, while cannon fire pressures Charlie behind walls where her yo-yo cannot reach.

  • Barley

    Attacks Charlie over walls and zones her movement, with lingering area damage clearing Spiders before they absorb shots.

  • Sandy

    Pierces Spiders with wide attacks, while Sandstorm concealment makes Charlie's narrow yo-yo and Cocoon harder to land.

  • Juju

    Lobs area damage over walls to clear Spiders, while Gris-Gris adds another target for Charlie's single yo-yo.

  • Larry

    Bombards Charlie over walls with two-stage area blasts that clear Spiders, while Lawrie forces extra yo-yo shots.

Also weak to: Thrower

Charlie is a strong pick against

  • Bolt

    Cocoons Bolt mid-charge, halting his approach despite the crowd-control immunity from his Super.

  • Chester

    Sends Spiders into Chester's bells, absorbing his multi-projectile burst before the summons pressure him up close.

  • Colette

    Uses Spiders to absorb Colette's non-piercing shots, then cocoons her before her charge completes its return hit.

  • Crow

    Forces Crow to spend repeated ammo clearing Spiders before his daggers can pressure her directly.

  • Gale

    Outranges Gale's snowballs, then cocoons him to remove his knockback from decisive team fights.

  • Gene

    Overloads Gene's slow reload with Spiders that intercept his shots and pull before Cocoon disables him.

  • Gray

    Clogs Gray's non-piercing shots with Spiders, then cocoons him before he can escape through a teleport.

  • Kenji

    Cocoons Kenji during his close-range pressure, blocking his attacks and healing while teammates prepare to punish his release.

  • Kit

    Cocoons Kit during his close-range commitment, isolating him before he can capitalize on the dive.

  • Leon

    Cocoons Leon as his ambush begins, isolating him before his close-range burst can finish its target.

  • Meeple

    Sends Spiders into Meeple's homing shots, forcing wasted ammo and denying clean hits.

  • Piper

    Overloads Piper's slow single-target reload with Spiders, then cocoons her to remove her long-range pressure.

  • Surge

    Outranges Surge before his range upgrade, while Spiders drain his slow reload without charging his Super.
