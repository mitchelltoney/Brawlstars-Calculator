Nori Counters

Best picks against Nori in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Nori

  • Bull

    Bursts Nori down at close range, then slows or stuns him when he tries to disengage.

  • Otis

    Silences Nori during his dive, blocking attacks and fish healing while leaving him exposed at close range.

  • Chester

    Bursts Nori at close range, while random crowd control can stun or slow him before he escapes.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Nori down at close range, then slows him with Super shells so he cannot disengage from the fight.

  • Gale

    Repels Nori's dive with Super knockback, then repeated slowing attacks prevent him from re-engaging.

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Nori at close range, then Tar Barrel's slow traps him inside Darryl's double-shot burst.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Nori's dive with high health, then stuns him before he can retreat or heal with fish.

  • Buzz

    Stuns Nori as he dives, denying his attacks long enough to survive and unload burst damage at close range.

Also weak to: Tank Knockback
