Best counters to Lola

  • Bea

    Outranges Lola and alternates normal and Supercharged shots, breaking her firing lane before the Ego establishes crossfire.

  • Belle

    Outranges Lola, and Electro-Bolts bounce between her and a nearby Ego, multiplying pressure whenever they stack together.

  • Bo

    Hits Lola and her Ego together with exploding arrows, while hidden mines deny the stationary lanes her sustained damage needs.

  • Poco

    Out-sustains Lola while wide piercing attacks damage her and the Ego together, negating the clone's body-blocking value.

  • Penny

    Punishes Lola for stacking with her Ego, splashing gold pouches through the clone to damage both targets.

  • Amber

    Pierces through Lola's Ego with sustained flames, damaging both targets and denying the clone's value as a shield.
