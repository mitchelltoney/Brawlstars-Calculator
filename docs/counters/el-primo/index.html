El Primo Counters

Best picks against El Primo in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to El Primo

  • Cordelius

    Silences his close-range punches with Poison Mushroom, then unloads high burst before he can retaliate.

  • Gale

    Keeps his punches out of reach with slows and Super knockback, repeatedly denying every attempt to close distance.

  • Shelly

    Bursts through his tank health at point-blank range, then Super knockback pushes his punches out of reach.

  • Lumi

    Outranges his punches with long-range morning stars, then recalled-star slows keep him from reaching her between throws.

El Primo is a strong pick against

  • Bibi

    Outlasts Bibi up close, then uses his Super to reengage after her charged swing knocks him away.

  • Buster

    Overwhelms Buster in sustained melee with faster-reloading punches, using a leap to erase his range advantage.

  • Fang

    Outlasts Fang's melee combo, then overwhelms his slower kick sequence with faster-reloading punches.

  • Finx

    Jumps directly onto Finx, bypassing Time Warp's projectile slowdown and overwhelming him with close-range punches.

  • Frank

    Punishes Frank's stationary windups at point-blank range, while Suplex Supplement cancels his Super before more punches follow.

  • Juju

    Leaps over wall cover to reach Juju, converting her artillery advantage into a close-range fight his durability favors.

  • Kenji

    Outlasts Kenji at close range, where rapid punches overwhelm the healing built into his alternating attacks.

  • Trunk

    Out-damages Trunk at close range, while Suplex Supplement interrupts the delayed spin before it connects.
