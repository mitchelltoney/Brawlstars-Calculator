Best counters to 8bit

  • Penny

    Pressures his stationary Damage Booster from artillery range, while Salty Barrel blocks his projectiles and enables pouch bursts.

  • Brock

    Destroys cover around his Damage Booster with Rocket Fuel, exposing the setup to sustained long-range rocket pressure.

  • Squeak

    Zones the area around his Damage Booster with Sticky Blomb explosions, forcing him away from its damage boost.

  • Piper

    Punishes 8bit's slow movement and spread at long range, where her shots peak in damage and his accuracy falls.

  • Belle

    Disrupts his long-range volleys by marking him to stop reloading, breaking the sustained fire his Damage Booster rewards.
