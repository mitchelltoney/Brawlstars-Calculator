8bit Counters

Best picks against 8bit in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to 8bit

  • Penny

    Pressures his stationary Damage Booster from artillery range, while Salty Barrel blocks his projectiles and enables pouch bursts.

  • Brock

    Destroys cover around his Damage Booster with Rocket Fuel, exposing the setup to sustained long-range rocket pressure.

  • Squeak

    Zones the area around his Damage Booster with Sticky Blomb explosions, forcing him away from its damage boost.

  • Piper

    Punishes his slow movement with maximum-damage shots from long range, making every missed dodge especially costly.

  • Belle

    Uses Grounded to halt his reloading after a mark, disrupting the sustained fire his Damage Booster rewards.

8bit is a strong pick against

  • Otis

    Outranges Otis and wins direct damage trades with greater health and six-beam volleys, denying him safe Super charge.

  • Pam

    Punishes Pam's inaccurate long-range Scrapstorm with superior reach, then overwhelms her turret's gradual healing with Damage Booster.

  • Pearl

    Absorbs Pearl's volleys with greater health and answers from longer range, forcing her to spend Heat before closing.
