Spike Counters

Best picks against Spike in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Spike

  • Squeak

    Out-controls Spike around cover with delayed sticky explosions, forcing him away from every contested choke.

  • Carl

    Out-tanks Spike's chip and pressures his low health with pickaxe hits on both the outward and return paths.

  • Piper

    Outranges Spike with maximum-damage shots, punishing his low health before his cactus grenades can threaten her lane.

Also weak to: Assassin Tank Thrower

Spike is a strong pick against

  • Crow

    Catches Crow's evasive movement with Curveball spikes, turning reliable hits into heavy pressure against his low health.

  • Meeple

    Pressures Meeple around cover with split spikes while Life Plant absorbs their single-projectile attacks.

  • Nani

    Lets Life Plant absorb Nani's focused shots and heal nearby teammates when destroyed.

  • Shelly

    Keeps Shelly outside shotgun range, landing split spikes before she can deliver concentrated close-range damage.

  • Stu

    Places Life Plant between Stu and his non-piercing fireworks, denying the hits he needs to chain Super dashes.

  • Surge

    Outranges Surge before his range upgrade while Life Plant absorbs shots that would charge his Super.
