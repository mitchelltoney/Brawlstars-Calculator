Best picks against Mina in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Mina

  • Kenji

    Bypasses Mina's projectile wall with dash-slash attacks, then Hosomaki Healing recovers recent combo damage.

  • Mortis

    Outmaneuvers Mina's sequenced attacks with repeated dashes, then punishes the empty-ammo window after her delayed third strike.

  • Stu

    Outmaneuvers Mina's shrinking three-step combo with chained dashes, forcing misses before her powerful third attack can connect.

  • Shade

    Ignores Mina's projectile wall with attacks that pass through cover, while Hardened Hoodie blunts her close-range combo.

  • Meeple

    Outranges Mina's later combo stages with long homing projectiles, then Ragequit interrupts her dash-ins with stun and knockback.

Mina is a strong pick against

  • Trunk

    Uses attack dashes to kite Trunk's short spin, then interrupts Trunk's windup with a Super before damage lands.
