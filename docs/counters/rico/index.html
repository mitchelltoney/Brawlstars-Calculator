Rico Counters

Best picks against Rico in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Rico

  • Shelly

    Closes on Rico with Fast Forward, fully reloads, then overwhelms his low health with concentrated shotgun damage.

  • Emz

    Attacks through Rico's wall cover with Acid Spray, denying the protected bounce angles that empower his bullets.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Rico's bullet pressure, then Irresistible Attraction pulls him away from bounce angles and into close-range swings.

  • Brock

    Destroys the wall geometry Rico relies on with Rocket Fuel, removing empowered bounces and forcing exposed, direct fights.

  • Piper

    Outranges Rico in open lanes, while Auto Aimer pushes him away if he closes past her marksman advantage.

  • Stu

    Dashes around Rico's bouncing fire and uses Breakthrough to destroy his strongest wall angles while maintaining close pressure.

  • Mandy

    Outranges Rico while Focused, and Cookie Crumbs pierces his wall cover before his bouncing shots can pressure her.

Also weak to: Thrower
