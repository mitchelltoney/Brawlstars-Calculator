Rosa Counters

Best picks against Rosa in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Rosa

  • Buzz

    Out-bursts Rosa at close range, while his stun interrupts her slow punch sequence and wastes part of her shield.

  • Sprout

    Outranges Rosa's punches with arcing Seed Bombs, then blocks her approach with Hedge walls she cannot quickly bypass.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Rosa at point-blank range and cycles Super knockbacks, repeatedly interrupting her sustained punching pressure.

  • Emz

    Punishes Rosa's approach with lingering spray damage, then Friendzoner pushes her back outside punching range.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Rosa with superior health, repeatedly stunning her while his Super destroys the bushes supporting her approach.

Also weak to: Assassin

Rosa is a strong pick against

  • Ash

    Absorbs Ash's Rage-fueled damage with her Super, then wins the close-range exchange with continued punches.

  • Buster

    Uses her Super to blunt Buster's Lens Flare, then exploits her faster reload to keep punches coming at close range.

  • Crow

    Endures Crow's poison and close-range dagger burst behind her Super, creating enough time to connect repeated punches.

  • Hank

    Shields against Hank's Balloon Blast at point-blank range, then overwhelms him with rapid punches.

  • Jacky

    Reduces Jacky's Groundbreaker damage with her Super, then keeps punching until the extended close-range exchange swings in her favor.
