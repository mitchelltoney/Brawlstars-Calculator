Best picks against Pearl in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Pearl

  • Jessie

    Forces Pearl to waste heated volleys on Scrappy, draining the resource that powers her burst before Jessie is exposed.

  • Kenji

    Pressures Pearl before Heat builds, alternating dashes and slashes while healing through repeated close-range engagements.

  • Bo

    Controls Pearl’s advance with hidden mines, while Snare A Bear stuns her before she unloads heated cookies.

  • Lou

    Out-controls Pearl by stacking freeze, while Hypothermia suppresses her heated damage until a full freeze interrupts the next volley.

  • 8bit

    Out-tanks Pearl’s volleys with massive health and answers from longer range, forcing her to spend Heat before closing.

  • Buster

    Blocks Pearl’s heated cookie volleys with Montage, converting each intercepted projectile into counterfire before her strongest pressure can connect.

Pearl is a strong pick against

  • Larry

    Absorbs Larry's paired blasts behind Heat Shield, then spends stored Heat on a counterattack that punishes both twins.
