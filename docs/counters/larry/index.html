Larry Counters

Best picks against Larry in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Larry

  • Crow

    Outmaneuvers Larry's delayed explosions, keeping him poisoned so retreat healing stalls and every exposed peek costs more health.

  • Stu

    Dodges Larry's delayed explosions with repeated dashes, closes through his artillery range, and keeps pressure between throws.

  • Pearl

    Out-tanks Larry's double explosions with Heat Shield, then converts stored Heat into burst that overwhelms both twins.

  • Tick

    Outranges Larry with lingering mines, lobs over walls, and controls retreat paths before he can reposition.

  • Edgar

    Bypasses Larry's artillery range with a jump, then rapid lifestealing punches overwhelm him before delayed blasts detonate.

  • Kenji

    Dashes around Larry's delayed blasts, closes the artillery gap, and recovers recent damage with Hosomaki Healing.

Larry is a strong pick against

  • Charlie

    Hits Charlie behind cover with overlapping blast stages, clearing Spiders as Lawrie draws away her yo-yo attacks.

  • Emz

    Attacks Emz beyond her spray range with double explosions while Lawrie pressures her vulnerable close-range zone.

  • Finx

    Pins Finx between Lawrie's flank pressure and double blasts over cover, making Time Warp less secure.

  • Juju

    Makes Juju spend ground throws on Lawrie while longer-ranged explosives keep landing from beyond her reach.

  • Moe

    Redirects some of Moe's bouncing damage into Lawrie, preserving health through sustained pressure.

  • Sandy

    Dislodges Sandy from protected positions using longer-ranged blasts over walls as Lawrie threatens from another direction.

  • Willow

    Punishes Willow's slow reload with consecutive explosions while Lawrie makes her divide ammunition between both twins.
