Cordelius Counters

Best picks against Cordelius in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Cordelius

  • Frank

    Outranges and out-tanks Cordelius, surviving paired mushroom bursts and punishing every close approach with heavy hammer swings.

  • Buster

    Out-tanks Cordelius at equal range, absorbing mushroom volleys before pulling him back into the next close-range blast.

  • Surge

    Bursts Cordelius down in three shots during Shadow Realm duels, ending the fight before Cordelius's reload advantage matters.

  • Nita

    Outranges Cordelius with wide shockwaves and uses Bruce to absorb paired mushrooms, forcing his limited ammo into a durable summon.

Cordelius is a strong pick against

  • Bibi

    Controls Bibi before she closes the gap with dual projectiles, reserving Poison Mushroom to prevent a point-blank counterattack.

  • Chuck

    Sends Chuck into the Shadow Realm, cutting him off from his established Post network, Super travel, and objective pressure.

  • Damian

    Stops Damian's short-range punches with Poison Mushroom when he closes in, creating a burst window without retaliation.

  • Darryl

    Exploits Darryl's missing Barrel Roll inside the Shadow Realm, using its movement and reload boosts to remain outside shotgun range.

  • El Primo

    Uses Poison Mushroom after El Primo reaches punching range, denying his response while mushroom bursts punish the approach.

  • Kenji

    Uses Shadow Realm isolation against Kenji to lock his Super and gadgets, then stays beyond the reach of his alternating attacks.

  • Kit

    Turns Kit's team-oriented role into a weakness by forcing him to fight alone inside the Shadow Realm.

  • Meg

    Prevents Meg from rebuilding her Mecha inside the Shadow Realm, giving repeated two-shot bursts time to exhaust each form.

  • Melodie

    Forces Melodie into a stripped-down close-range duel, since the Shadow Realm clears her orbiting notes and weakens her dive.

  • Ruffs

    Pulls Ruffs away from his team, using close-range mushroom fire to exploit his low health throughout the Shadow Realm duel.
