Bibi Counters

Best picks against Bibi in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Bibi

  • Surge

    Outranges Bibi's bat, then uses his Super to dodge delayed swings and knock her back when she closes.

  • El Primo

    Out-tanks Bibi up close, then leaps back in after her charged swing knocks him away and unloads rapid punches.

  • Cordelius

    Outranges Bibi's bat with paired mushroom bursts, while Poison Mushroom silences her attack when she finally reaches him.

  • Shelly

    Bursts down Bibi's tank health at point-blank range, with Super knockback interrupting her delayed swing and resetting distance.

Also weak to: Knockback

Bibi is a strong pick against

  • Mr P

    Clears Mr P's porters with piercing bat swings, then uses Home Run to catch him before replacements accumulate.

  • Tara

    Outlasts Tara's slow-reloading card volleys, rushes through her range, and sweeps clustered Shadows with wide swings.

  • Tick

    Crosses Tick's delayed minefield with high health and fast movement, then overwhelms his low health up close.
