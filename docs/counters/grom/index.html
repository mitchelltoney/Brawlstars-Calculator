Grom Counters

Best picks against Grom in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Grom

  • Piper

    Punishes Grom's slow lobbed attacks with fast, direct shots, sidestepping each explosion before he establishes artillery pressure.

  • Mr P

    Reaches Grom behind cover with bouncing suitcases, forcing him from walls before he can establish artillery control.

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Grom and rolls directly onto him, forcing a close-range fight where his delayed explosives rarely connect.

  • Mico

    Jumps over Grom's delayed explosives, lands beside him, and stays too mobile for reliable point-blank defense.

  • Mortis

    Closes on Grom with repeated dashes, weaving around cross-shaped blasts and punishing his weak point-blank defense.

  • Kenji

    Closes on Grom with his dash-slash attacks, then heals recent blast damage while pressuring his weak point-blank defense.

  • Kaze

    Outmaneuvers Grom with a Ninja Aspect speed boost, crossing his artillery range before the delayed explosives land.

  • Edgar

    Closes instantly behind Grom with his scarf jump, bypassing artillery range and sustaining through weak point-blank damage.

Also weak to: Assassin

Grom is a strong pick against

  • Lumi

    Lobs cross-shaped blasts over walls to pressure Lumi while cover blocks her forward-thrown morning stars.
