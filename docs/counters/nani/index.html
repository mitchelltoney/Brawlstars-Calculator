Nani Counters

Best picks against Nani in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Nani

  • Spike

    Absorbs Nani's focused marksman damage with Life Plant cover, which heals nearby teammates when destroyed.

  • Griff

    Overwhelms Nani's single-hit Return To Sender with multi-coin volleys, preserving most of his burst after one coin reflects.

  • Gene

    Punishes Nani's long-range positioning with homing missiles that target every visible opponent and scale damage with distance.

  • Max

    Outmaneuvers Nani's precision shots with superior speed, while faster reloads during movement sustain pressure between dodges.

  • Darryl

    Closes the gap on Nani with Barrel Roll, then Tar Barrel's slow keeps her trapped under close-range pressure.

  • Tick

    Bombards Nani from behind walls, denying clean marksman sightlines while shorter reloads keep mines covering her approach.

Also weak to: Thrower

Nani is a strong pick against

  • Angelo

    Turns Angelo's fully charged arrow against them with Return To Sender, reflecting part of the heavy hit.

  • Belle

    Outranges Belle once the orbs converge, threatening a three-projectile burst that drives her from open sniper lanes.

  • Brock

    Outranges Brock and uses Return To Sender to reflect part of his heavy rocket damage.

  • Maisie

    Outranges Maisie with converging projectiles, forcing her forward before Shockwave can threaten the lane.

  • Mandy

    Turns Mandy's telegraphed Super against her with Return To Sender, reflecting much of its damage.

  • Penny

    Outranges Penny's gold pouches and bursts her down before the cannon establishes sustained map pressure.

  • Piper

    Outranges Piper after the orbs converge and uses Return To Sender to punish one heavy sniper round with reflected damage.
