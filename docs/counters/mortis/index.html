Mortis Counters

Best picks against Mortis in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Mortis

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Mortis in melee and returns part of every dash's damage through Counter Crush before he can disengage.

  • Shade

    Outlasts Mortis's dash combo through Incorporeal damage reduction, forcing him to spend extra ammo before he can escape.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Mortis at his preferred range, then Stomper keeps him slowed inside Bull's shotgun burst.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Mortis at shotgun range, then slows him with Super shells before he can dash back out.

  • Gale

    Repels Mortis's dives with Super knockback, and Blustery Blow stuns him against obstacles before he can dash away.

  • Otis

    Stops Mortis's close-range assault by preventing attacks, leaving him unable to chain dashes or finish weakened targets.

  • Chester

    Bursts down Mortis during his close-range commitment, using sequenced bell damage before he can complete another dash.

  • Crow

    Kites Mortis outside dash range, then Slowing Toxin denies the movement he needs to chain attacks or retreat.
