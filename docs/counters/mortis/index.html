Mortis Counters

Best picks against Mortis in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Mortis

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Mortis in melee and returns part of every dash's damage through Counter Crush before he can disengage.

  • Shade

    Outlasts Mortis's dash combo with Hardened Hoodie, forcing him to spend extra ammo before he can escape.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Mortis at melee range, then Stomper keeps him slowed inside concentrated shotgun blasts.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Mortis at shotgun range, then Shell Shock slows him before he can dash back out.

  • Gale

    Repels Mortis's dives with Super knockback, and Blustery Blow stuns him against obstacles before he can dash away.

  • Otis

    Stops Mortis's close-range assault by preventing attacks, leaving him unable to chain dashes or finish weakened targets.

  • Chester

    Bursts down Mortis during his close-range commitment, using sequenced bell damage before he can complete another dash.

  • Crow

    Kites Mortis outside dash range, then Slowing Toxin denies the movement he needs to chain attacks or retreat.

Mortis is a strong pick against

  • Barley

    Slips between Barley's slow bottle arcs with dashes and punishes his low health before another throw becomes available.

  • Berry

    Skirts Berry's persistent puddles with dashes, then exploits his low health during the lengthy reload between cones.

  • Dynamike

    Cuts across Dynamike's timed explosions with dashes and reaches melee range, punishing his low health before a retaliatory throw is possible.

  • Emz

    Enters the dead zone inside Emz's spray with a dash, limiting each cloud to one hit before closing the duel.

  • Grom

    Uses consecutive dashes through gaps in Grom's cross pattern, forcing the artillery fighter to defend at melee distance.

  • Leon

    Evades Leon's blade volleys through attack dashes, then activates Combo Spinner to secure the close-range damage race.

  • Lou

    Slips between Lou's narrow volleys with attack dashes and begins melee pressure before the freeze meter builds.

  • Mandy

    Chains dashes past Mandy's range, exploiting her low health and lack of close-range knockback.

  • Mina

    Times repeated dashes around Mina's attack sequence and strikes during the ammo gap after her delayed third hit.

  • Mr P

    Breaches Mr P's porter screen with dashes, bypasses bouncing suitcases, and forces the controller into close combat.

  • Nita

    Threads attack dashes between Nita's shockwaves and bear, bypassing both to reach her directly.

  • Poco

    Dives through Poco's low-burst sound waves, then drains grouped enemies with a piercing, self-healing Super.

  • Sirius

    Carves through Sirius's clustered Shadows during each dash, turning piercing shovel swings into direct access to their summoner.

  • Sprout

    Navigates Sprout's Seed Bombs with attack dashes, collapsing the gap before the artillery thrower can disengage.

  • Squeak

    Dashes around Squeak's delayed blast zones, then maintains point-blank pressure while his slow reload denies an immediate answer.

  • Tick

    Navigates Tick's delayed minefield with dashes, then exploits his low health at point-blank range before the mines detonate.

  • Ziggy

    Crosses Ziggy's telegraphed lightning areas with dashes and maintains melee pressure before his next strike resolves.
