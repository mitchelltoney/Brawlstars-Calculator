Amber Counters

Best picks against Amber in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Amber

  • Carl

    Withstands Amber's sustained fire as Protective Pirouette reduces damage during his Super, maintaining pressure through her flames.

  • Pam

    Denies Amber's continuous fire with Scrapsucker, draining her ammo while replenishing Pam's reserves.

  • Crow

    Cuts Amber's sustained flame damage with Extra Toxic, weakening her continuous-fire pressure throughout every poisoned trade.

  • Piper

    Outranges Amber and deals maximum damage from that distance before her continuous flames enter their effective range.

  • Belle

    Outranges Amber and uses Grounded to halt her reload, preventing continuous fire from rebuilding pressure.

Also weak to: Sniper

Amber is a strong pick against

  • Chester

    Pressures Chester with sustained fire during his slow reload windows while staying beyond the range where clustered bells connect.

  • Frank

    Burns Frank throughout his attack windup from beyond hammer range, maintaining continuous damage without entering his reach.

  • Gale

    Wins ranged trades against Gale through continuous fire while his wide spread returns only partial damage.

  • Lily

    Keeps Lily outside attack range, then punishes her forced point-blank teleport with Dancing Flames.

  • Lola

    Pierces Lola and her Ego in one sustained flame stream, denying the clone's value as a shield.

  • Sirius

    Burns Sirius and his clustered Shadows in the same piercing flame stream, dismantling the protective swarm.
