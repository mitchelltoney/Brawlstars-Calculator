Best picks against Moe in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Moe

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Moe's bouncing attacks, using Hardy Hard Hat to reduce each hit throughout sustained exchanges.

  • Shade

    Punishes Moe's Driller approach by slowing him nearby with Jump Scare, denying the movement needed to disengage.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Moe's bouncing shots, gaining a Tough Guy shield at low health to survive his sustained pressure.

  • Shelly

    Punishes Moe's Driller approach with shotgun pressure, while Shell Shock slows him and denies an immediate retreat.

  • Gale

    Denies Moe's Driller approach with Super knockback, while Blustery Blow stuns him whenever obstacles stop the push.

  • Penny

    Denies Moe's bouncing projectiles with Salty Barrel, which also lets her burst gold pouches into his approach.

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Moe's sustained pressure, using Steel Hoops after Barrel Roll to absorb damage while staying on top of him.

  • Larry

    Out-sustains Moe's bouncing pressure by transferring incoming damage to nearby Lawrie, splitting his shots across both twins.
