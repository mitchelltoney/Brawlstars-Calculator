Gus Counters

Best picks against Gus in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Gus

  • Dynamike

    Lobs explosives over walls, denying Gus clean sightlines and pressuring him without entering his direct firing lane.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks Gus, then pulls him into melee range where heavy swings overwhelm his limited durability.

  • Gale

    Controls Gus's spacing with repeated slows, then pushes him away whenever he tries to collect healing spirits.

  • Piper

    Outranges Gus and deals greater damage at maximum distance, winning repeated peeks before his spirits can provide sustained healing.

  • Mr P

    Floods Gus with recurring porters, consuming his shots while suitcase explosions keep pressuring him around cover.

  • Darryl

    Closes Gus's range with Barrel Roll, gains damage reduction on arrival, and bursts him down up close.

Also weak to: Knockback

Gus is a strong pick against

  • Colt

    Outlasts Colt's long volleys with higher health and a Super shield capable of absorbing an entire attack.

  • Crow

    Outranges Crow and shields through poison chip, winning repeated balloon trades before Crow's Super forces close-range combat.

  • Finx

    Outranges Finx while spirit healing and Spooky's shield absorb his chip throughout prolonged lane trades.

  • Gene

    Outtrades Gene's split-projectile chip, then counters his pull with a damage-absorbing shield and point-blank knockback.

  • Gray

    Slightly outranges Gray's narrow shot and outlasts his poke through spirit healing and Spooky's shield.

  • Mandy

    Outlasts Mandy's single-shot poke through spirit healing, while a Super shield absorbs her burst damage.

  • Max

    Outranges Max's four-projectile bursts and outlasts her chip damage through healing spirits and Spooky's shield.

  • Poco

    Outranges Poco's low-damage waves, absorbs his return fire with Spooky's shield, and lands focused single-target shots.

  • RT

    Outlasts RT's ranged chip through spirit healing, with Health Bonanza strengthening recovery between marked follow-up shots.
