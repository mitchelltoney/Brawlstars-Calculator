Otis Counters

Best picks against Otis in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Otis

  • Jessie

    Uses Energize to keep her turret absorbing Otis's non-piercing volleys, forcing him to waste ammo while she maintains equal-range pressure.

  • Lou

    Controls Otis with freeze buildup, reducing his damage through Hypothermia and interrupting his ink volleys with stuns.

  • Colette

    Out-sustains Otis in repeated midrange trades, dealing current-health damage while Gotcha heals through return fire.

  • 8bit

    Outranges Otis and wins direct damage trades with higher health and six-beam volleys, denying safe Super charge.

  • Ruffs

    Uses Take Cover to absorb Otis's non-piercing shots and Super while wall-bouncing lasers maintain pressure from safety.

  • Chester

    Punishes Otis at close range with converging bell volleys, delivering heavier burst before his ink attack finishes unloading.

  • Poco

    Cleanses Otis's silence from allies and grants them temporary immunity to further debuffs with Protective Tunes.

  • Alli

    Ambushes Otis from stealth, then chains short dash attacks with enraged speed before he can land his silence.

Otis is a strong pick against

  • Darryl

    Keeps Darryl outside shotgun range, then silences his post-roll burst before he can fire.

  • Dynamike

    Out-pokes Dynamike with long-range ink, then silences his attacks and Satchel Charge after he leaves cover.

  • Edgar

    Silences Edgar on landing, denying his punches and their healing during the duel.

  • Fang

    Silences Fang during his dive, preventing attacks and follow-up Supers while sustained ink volleys finish him.

  • Mortis

    Silences Mortis at close range, preventing chained dashes and leaving weakened targets beyond his reach.

  • Nori

    Silences Nori during his dive, blocking attacks and fish-based healing while exposing him to close-range ink.

  • Ollie

    Outranges Ollie's soundwaves and silences him before Ollie can begin a Super dive or follow-up attack.

  • Starr Nova

    Silences Starr Nova at close range, stopping sword-form attacks and self-healing while extra ink drops complete the burst.

  • Tara

    Outranges Tara and her card spread, then silences her attacks and Super before she can retaliate.
