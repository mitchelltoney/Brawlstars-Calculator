Otis Counters

Best picks against Otis in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Otis

  • Jessie

    Overloads Otis's non-piercing volleys with a repairable turret, forcing him to waste ammo while Jessie maintains equal-range pressure.

  • Lou

    Controls Otis with freeze buildup, reducing his damage through Hypothermia and interrupting his ink volleys with stuns.

  • Colette

    Out-sustains Otis in repeated midrange trades, stripping his current health while Gotcha heals through return fire.

  • 8bit

    Outranges Otis and wins straight damage trades with higher health and six-beam burst, denying safe silence charge.

  • Ruffs

    Controls the lane through cover, letting sandbags absorb Otis's ink and silence while wall-bouncing lasers maintain pressure.

  • Chester

    Bursts Otis at close range with converging bell volleys, overwhelming him before his spread ink attack can trade efficiently.

  • Poco

    Negates Otis's silence by cleansing the adverse effect and granting allies temporary immunity with Protective Tunes.

  • Alli

    Overwhelms Otis after ambushing from invisibility, using dash mobility and enraged reload speed to finish before he can silence her.
