Frank Counters

Best picks against Frank in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Frank

  • Janet

    Outranges Frank with fully focused shots, then takes flight before his delayed hammer or stunning Super can connect.

  • Amber

    Outranges Frank with continuous flames, maintaining damage through his windup while remaining beyond hammer range.

  • Penny

    Outranges Frank with long-range pouches, then Master Blaster can knock him back and cancel his windup when he closes.

  • Colette

    Shreds Frank’s huge health pool with percentage-based shots, then Push It can interrupt his Super and secure both charge hits.

  • El Primo

    Punishes Frank’s stationary windups at point-blank range, while Suplex Supplement can cancel his Super before renewed close-range pressure.

  • Griff

    Outranges Frank with coin volleys, then converts his forced approach into concentrated point-blank burst.

  • Chester

    Bursts Frank during his stationary windups with multi-bell volleys at close range, where every bell connects.

  • Lumi

    Exploits Frank’s stationary windups by throwing both morning stars past him, then damaging him again during their recall.

  • Rico

    Outranges Frank and ricochets shots around walls, punishing his stationary windups while remaining outside hammer range.

Also weak to: Thrower

Frank is a strong pick against

  • Ash

    Uses Sponge to weather Ash's Rage-fueled offense and win extended tank exchanges through superior durability.

  • Cordelius

    Keeps Cordelius beyond mushroom range, survives paired bursts, and punishes every approach with wide hammer swings.

  • Doug

    Pressures Doug with longer-reaching hammer waves while remaining beyond his compact circular attack.

  • Draco

    Checks Draco's advance with superior reach and stuns, preventing prolonged Dragon Solo pressure at close range.

  • Gus

    Drags Gus into hammer range, where superior durability and heavy swings overwhelm his limited health.

  • Hank

    Soaks Hank's charged Balloon Blast up close, then delivers heavy swings before his next attack finishes charging.

  • Jacky

    Uses Active Noise Canceling to resist Jacky's pull and wins the ensuing brawl through a larger health pool.

  • Janet

    Walks through Janet's focused shots, using superior durability to reach her before hammer swings force a retreat.

  • Jessie

    Strikes Jessie and Scrappy together with broad hammer waves, wearing down the turret before she can repair it.

  • Juju

    Absorbs combined pressure from Juju and Gris-Gris, then removes the doll with sweeping hammer attacks and advances.

  • Kenji

    Turns Kenji's close-range commitments against him, surviving sustained attacks before answering with heavy area damage.

  • Nori

    Tanks Nori's dive with high health, then lands a stun before he can disengage or consume fish for healing.

  • Rico

    After soaking Rico's bullet pressure, uses Irresistible Attraction to drag him from bounce lanes into hammer range.

  • Rosa

    Overpowers Rosa through a larger health pool, repeated stuns, and terrain destruction that strips away her bush cover.

  • Shade

    Combines longer reach with the durability to withstand Shade, then destroys wall cover that conceals its approach.

  • Sprout

    Withstands Sprout's Seed Bombs, then converts a distant pull into an immediate close-range hammer threat.

  • Starr Nova

    Survives Starr Nova's nearby burst through sheer health and punishes her before sword-form healing can reverse the exchange.

  • Trunk

    Threatens Trunk beyond spin range, withstands ant-enhanced damage up close, and stuns them before the exchange turns.
