Sandy Counters

Best picks against Sandy in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Sandy

  • Lou

    Freezes Sandy through repeated volleys, interrupting his low-damage attacks and denying sustained control inside the Sandstorm.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Sandy's low-damage blasts, then attacks through nearby walls where his Sandstorm positioning cannot keep him safe.

  • Nita

    Out-sustains Sandy's modest damage by cycling Bear With Me healing whenever she or her bear lands a hit.

  • Janet

    Outranges Sandy with fully focused shots, pressuring him before his medium-range pebbles can connect.

  • Kenji

    Out-sustains Sandy's gradual damage with Hosomaki Healing, then maintains close-range pressure through alternating dashes and slashes.

  • Larry

    Outranges Sandy with artillery over walls, forcing him from cover while Lawrie adds sustained pressure from another angle.

Also weak to: Tank

Sandy is a strong pick against

  • Charlie

    Clears Charlie's Spiders with wide, piercing attacks while Sandstorm concealment makes her narrow yo-yo and Cocoon difficult to aim.

  • Squeak

    Hides teammates from Squeak inside Sandstorm while wide attacks connect before his delayed Sticky Blombs can detonate.

  • Stu

    Tracks Stu's lateral dashes with wide, piercing attacks and uses Sandstorm concealment to interrupt reliable Super recharges.

  • Tara

    Hits Tara and her grouped Shadows with the same piercing attack, then offsets her slow reload through Healing Winds.
