Best picks against Sandy in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Sandy

  • Lou

    Freezes Sandy through repeated volleys, interrupting his low-damage attacks and denying sustained control inside the Sandstorm.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Sandy's low-damage blasts, then attacks through nearby walls where his Sandstorm positioning cannot keep him safe.

  • Nita

    Out-sustains Sandy's modest damage by cycling Bear With Me healing whenever she or her bear lands a hit.

  • Janet

    Outranges Sandy with fully focused shots, pressuring him before his medium-range pebbles can connect.

  • Kenji

    Out-sustains Sandy's gradual damage by healing recent wounds, then alternating dashes and slashes keeps constant close-range pressure.

  • Larry

    Outranges Sandy with artillery over walls, forcing him from cover while Lawrie adds sustained pressure from another angle.
