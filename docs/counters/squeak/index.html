Squeak Counters

Best picks against Squeak in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Squeak

  • Sandy

    Conceals his team inside Sandstorm, denying Squeak clean targets while wide attacks land before Sticky Blombs detonate.

  • Ash

    Out-tanks Squeak's delayed Sticky Blombs and builds Rage from their damage, gaining speed and damage to close the gap.

  • Kenji

    Dashes through gaps in Squeak's delayed zoning, then heals recent damage while overwhelming his slow reload at close range.

  • Mortis

    Dashes around Squeak's delayed explosion zones, then stays point-blank where slow reload leaves him without immediate defense.

  • Buzz

    Grapples onto Squeak from range and stuns him, unloading close-range burst before delayed Sticky Blombs can detonate.

  • Edgar

    Jumps behind Squeak to bypass his zoning, then heals through delayed blasts with rapid, close-range punches.

  • Darryl

    Rolls through Squeak's zoning and uses post-roll damage reduction to survive delayed blasts while unloading at point-blank range.

Also weak to: Healer Assassin

Squeak is a strong pick against

  • 8bit

    Bombards 8bit's Damage Booster position with delayed blasts, driving him outside the boosted area.

  • Janet

    Seeds Janet's firing angles with timed blasts, while Residue slows her movement before each focused shot.

  • Jessie

    Catches Jessie and Scrappy in overlapping delayed blasts, preventing her turret from holding a fixed lane.

  • Meeple

    Hits Meeple through cover with Sticky Blomb explosions, while Residue slows their escape from the blast area.

  • Penny

    Fills Penny's cannon position with timed blasts, pressuring her alongside it and breaking the setup her artillery depends on.

  • Shelly

    Controls space beyond Shelly's shotgun reach with timed blasts, and Residue hinders her push into close range.

  • Spike

    Drives Spike from cover by planting delayed area blasts across the chokepoints he needs to contest.
