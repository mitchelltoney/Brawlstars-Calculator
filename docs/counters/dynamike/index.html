Dynamike Counters

Best picks against Dynamike in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Dynamike

  • Mico

    Jumps over Dynamike's delayed explosives to close, then bursts down his low health before he can retaliate.

  • Mortis

    Dashes through Dynamike's delayed blasts to close instantly, leaving his low-health artillery frame exposed before he can retaliate.

  • Kenji

    Dashes past Dynamike's delayed blast zones, then uses rapid alternating strikes to overwhelm his low-health artillery frame.

  • Edgar

    Leaps onto Dynamike before delayed explosives detonate, then sustains through stray blast damage with rapid healing punches.

  • Colette

    Pressures Dynamike in open lanes with faster long-range projectiles, making his delayed lobs harder to land or trade.

  • Chester

    Bursts down Dynamike at close range, where clustered bells land before his delayed explosives can detonate.

  • Otis

    Out-pokes Dynamike with long-range ink bursts, then silence disables his attacks and Satchel Charge once he is exposed.

Also weak to: Thrower

Dynamike is a strong pick against

  • Gus

    Lobs explosives over walls to pressure Gus while staying outside the direct sightlines his shots require.

  • Hank

    Bombards Hank's predictable approach, while Satchel Charge interrupts Balloon Blast before he can release it.

  • Lumi

    Punishes Lumi from behind walls with burst damage that her forward-thrown morning stars cannot answer.
