Buzz Counters

Best picks against Buzz in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Buzz

  • Surge

    Knocks Buzz away with an invulnerable Super jump when he grapples in, stopping his close-range burst before it begins.

  • Gale

    Outranges Buzz, then interrupts Torpedo Throw with wide Super knockback before he can begin his close-range burst.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Buzz at point-blank range, while Counter Crush returns damage from every wave in his five-hit attack.

Buzz is a strong pick against

  • Buster

    Overwhelms Buster at point-blank range with five-hit bursts, using Torpedo Throw to approach and stun.

  • Carl

    Bursts down Carl at close range and interrupts his spinning Super with a stun before its damage builds.

  • Meg

    Closes through Meg's ranged Mecha pressure with a stun, then unloads five close-range hits before she can disengage.

  • Melodie

    Stops Melodie's dives with a stun and five-hit burst, denying her orbiting notes repeated contact.

  • Nori

    Stuns Nori during his dive, buying time to survive and unload close-range burst before he can attack.

  • Rosa

    Out-bursts Rosa up close, interrupting her slow punches with a stun and wasting part of her shield.

  • Ruffs

    Crosses Ruffs's long range with Torpedo Throw, then stuns and bursts him inside his sandbag cover.

  • Squeak

    Grapples onto Squeak from range, stunning and bursting him before his delayed Sticky Blombs can detonate.

  • Willow

    Grapples past Willow's wall cover, then stuns and bursts her before delayed poison can stack.
