Best picks against Damian in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Damian

  • Cordelius

    Silences Damian with Poison Mushroom, preventing his short-range punches during the window when he finally closes distance.

  • Colette

    Shreds Damian's tank health with percentage-based attacks, making his large health pool offer little protection.

  • Piper

    Outranges Damian's short punches and kites him across open lanes, forcing him through repeated shots before reaching her.

  • Gale

    Outranges Damian and uses Super knockback to deny the sustained contact his short punches require.

  • Belle

    Outranges Damian and kites his short-range punches, landing Electro-Bolts before he can close the gap.

  • Chester

    Bursts through Damian's tank health at close range, where later attack sequences concentrate multiple bells into him.

  • Stu

    Kites Damian's short range with Nitro Boost dashes, slipping beyond his punches whenever he tries to sustain contact.

Also weak to: Anti-Tank Sniper
