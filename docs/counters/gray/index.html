Best counters to Gray

  • Lola

    Overwhelms his narrow single shots with sustained jewel volleys, while her Ego absorbs bullets and attacks from a second angle.

  • Bea

    Outranges his narrow Finger Pistol, while wider shots and Supercharged follow-ups win the long-range poke exchange.

  • Stu

    Dodges his narrow shots with repeated dashes, then punishes portal arrivals with fast close-range burst.

  • Mr P

    Forces his non-piercing shots into porters, draining ammo while bouncing suitcases pressure Gray behind cover.

  • Charlie

    Clogs his non-piercing shots with spiders, then Cocoon disables Gray before he can teleport away.

  • Gus

    Slightly outranges his narrow shot and out-sustains Gray's poke through spirit healing and Spooky's shield.
