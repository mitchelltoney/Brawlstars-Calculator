Clancy Counters

Best picks against Clancy in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Clancy

  • Penny

    Denies Clancy's token scaling by blocking paintballs with Salty Barrel, while her cannon pressures him over cover.

  • Belle

    Outranges Clancy and reloads faster, denying the repeated hits he needs to earn tokens before reaching his stronger stages.

  • Tara

    Overloads Clancy's single-projectile opening stage with three attacking Shadows, forcing wasted ammo before closing for concentrated card damage.

Also weak to: Spawner

Clancy is a strong pick against

  • Byron

    Turns Byron's repeated frontline healing into upgrade progress, then breaks through his gradual sustain with stronger volleys.

  • Chuck

    Uses Chuck's fixed Post destinations as reliable upgrade fuel, building enough firepower to pressure his large health pool.

  • Darryl

    Converts Darryl's large health pool into upgrades, then catches his straight-line roll-ins with dense projectile volleys.

  • Doug

    Stays beyond Doug's circular attack while harvesting his high health for tokens that strengthen later volleys.

  • Fang

    Turns Fang's committed dives into reliable Super hits that accelerate the climb toward more powerful upgrade stages.

  • Meg

    Uses the longevity of Meg's Mecha to reach later stages, where denser firepower handles her second health bar as well.

  • Melodie

    Uses Melodie's dash commitments to connect multiple Super projectiles, accelerating token growth and increasing later attack pressure.
