Best counters to Sam

  • Nita

    Outnumbers Sam at close range with her bear, then stuns him to punish each committed melee exchange.

  • Janet

    Outranges Sam's short punches, then uses Backstage Pass to retreat over walls when he closes the gap.

  • Kenji

    Out-sustains Sam in melee because Hosomaki Healing restores recent damage after a close-range exchange.

  • Surge

    Denies Sam's close-range dives with a knockback Super, then upgrades movement speed and range to keep him out.

  • Lou

    Stops Sam's rushes by freezing and stunning him, while accumulated frost reduces the damage of his close-range punches.

  • Griff

    Bursts down Sam at close range, where the wide coin attack and returning Super punish every approach.
