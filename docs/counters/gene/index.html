Gene Counters

Best picks against Gene in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Gene

  • Gus

    Outtrades Gene's split-projectile chip, then answers a pull with a damage-absorbing shield and point-blank knockback.

  • Mr P

    Overwhelms Gene's slow reload with recurring porters that absorb his single-target shots and intercept his pull.

  • Penny

    Blocks Gene's shots and pull with Salty Barrel, denying the isolation his team uses to secure kills.

  • Tara

    Overruns Gene's slow single-target fire with three shadows that absorb his shots and intercept his pull.

  • Eve

    Outranges Gene's full-damage shot and floods his slow reload with hatchlings that absorb attacks and intercept his pull.

  • Charlie

    Overwhelms Gene's slow reload with three spiders that block his shots and pull before Cocoon disables him.

  • Belle

    Outranges Gene's full-damage projectile and punishes allies clustering for his healing with bolts that bounce between them.

Also weak to: Spawner

Gene is a strong pick against

  • Carl

    Interrupts Carl's spinning dive with Lamp Blowout, pushing him away before his Super can deal sustained damage.

  • Glowy

    Chips Glowy from beyond glowbeam range, then pulls him from teammates to break his support tether.

  • Jae-Yong

    Pulls Jae-Yong away from nearby teammates, removing his stacking speed boosts and exposing him to focused damage.

  • Mr P

    Sustains teammates through Mr P's porter pressure with Magic Puffs, denying his spawnables the chip damage they need.

  • Najia

    Keeps teammates healthy through Najia's poison with Magic Puffs, preventing her damage over time from securing eliminations.

  • Nani

    Punishes Nani's distant positioning with Vengeful Spirits, whose homing missiles gain damage across long travel distances.

  • Piper

    Pulls Piper out of her preferred long-range lane, forcing close combat where her distance-scaled damage falls sharply.
