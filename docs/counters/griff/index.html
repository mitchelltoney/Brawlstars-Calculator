Griff Counters

Best picks against Griff in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Griff

  • Stu

    Dashes through gaps in his spreading coin volleys, repeatedly poking before Griff can connect every wave.

  • Ruffs

    Outranges his coin volley and uses sandbags to absorb non-piercing coins, denying Griff clean shots and close-range burst.

  • Chester

    Bursts through his moderate health with later bell sequences before Griff's extended coin volley fully connects.

  • Meeple

    Pressures Griff with slightly homing projectiles, then stuns and knocks him back before his close-range burst fully connects.

  • Max

    Outmaneuvers his spreading coin volleys with superior speed, sustaining pressure through four ammo slots and a faster reload.

Also weak to: Assassin

Griff is a strong pick against

  • Bull

    Shreds Bull's tank health with concentrated close-range coins while Piggy Bank removes the cover he needs to approach.

  • Colette

    Outlasts Colette's percentage-based pressure with Business Resilience, then punishes her predictable charge return with concentrated close-range coins.

  • Doug

    Outranges Doug's circular attack, then concentrates coin volleys to shred his high health when he closes the distance.

  • Edgar

    Concentrates every coin on Edgar at point-blank range, overwhelming his punch healing before it can stabilize the duel.

  • Fang

    Punishes Fang's committed dives with dense coin volleys that concentrate into heavy burst at point-blank range.

  • Frank

    Outranges Frank with coin volleys, then turns his forced approach into concentrated point-blank burst.

  • Nani

    Overwhelms Nani's Return To Sender with multi-coin volleys, preserving most of the burst after only one coin reflects.

  • Ruffs

    Bursts down Ruffs at midrange while Piggy Bank removes the walls that amplify his bouncing laser pressure.

  • Sam

    Bursts down Sam at close range, where wide coin volleys and the returning Super punish every approach.
