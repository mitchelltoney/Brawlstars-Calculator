Bonnie Counters

Best picks against Bonnie in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Bonnie

  • Darryl

    Out-tanks Bonnie's cannon form and rolls through her range, gaining damage reduction before forcing a close-range fight.

  • RT

    Outranges Bonnie's cannon form and amplifies follow-up damage through marks, winning ranged trades before she can engage.

  • Colette

    Punishes Bonnie's high-health cannon form with percentage-based attacks, converting her durability into heavier opening damage.

  • Piper

    Outranges Bonnie's cannon form, then knocks her back if she transforms and tries to force a close-range fight.

Also weak to: Sniper

Bonnie is a strong pick against

  • Glowy

    Outranges and out-tanks Glowy's glowbeam in cannon form, then launches onto him for close-range burst.

  • Najia

    Pressures Najia from marksman range, then uses Sugar Rush to exploit downtime between her single-ammo attacks.
