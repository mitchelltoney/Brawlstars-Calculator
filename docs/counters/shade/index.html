Shade Counters

Best picks against Shade in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Shade

  • Lou

    Controls Shade's short-range approach with freeze buildup, repeatedly interrupting its pressure before center hits can connect.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Shade at point-blank range and attacks through walls, denying the cover its short range depends on.

  • Frank

    Out-tanks and outranges Shade's short swings, then destroys wall cover with his Super to expose its approach.

Also weak to: Tank

Shade is a strong pick against

  • Mina

    Bypasses Mina's projectile wall with cover-piercing attacks while Hardened Hoodie reduces her close-range combo damage.

  • Moe

    Meets Moe's Driller approach with Jump Scare, slowing his retreat and limiting his ability to disengage.

  • Mortis

    Endures Mortis's dash combo through Hardened Hoodie, forcing extra ammo before he can escape.
