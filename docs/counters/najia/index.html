Best picks against Najia in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Najia

  • Bonnie

    Pressures Najia from marksman range, then Sugar Rush's movement and reload boosts exploit downtime between her single-ammo attacks.

  • Gene

    Out-sustains Najia's poison by healing nearby teammates every second, preventing her damage over time from securing eliminations.

  • Belle

    Cripples Najia's single-ammo cycle with Grounded, preventing reloads so she cannot launch another snake or extend her Super.

  • Barley

    Out-sustains Najia's poison with Medical Use, healing on every artillery throw while pressuring her safely over walls.

  • Eve

    Overwhelms Najia's single-ammo pressure with disposable hatchlings, which cannot trigger Poisonous Protector when defeated.

Najia is a strong pick against

  • Pierce

    Outranges Pierce with redirected snake shots and layers poison damage through his slow reload cycles.
