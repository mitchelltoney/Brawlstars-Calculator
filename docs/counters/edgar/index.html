Edgar Counters

Best picks against Edgar in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Edgar

  • Surge

    Knocks Edgar away on landing with a precharged Super, denying punch healing and creating room for follow-up shots.

  • Doug

    Out-sustains Edgar at point-blank range because Self Service heals every attack while Doug's revival punishes an all-in.

  • Jacky

    Out-tanks Edgar's close-range punches and returns part of their damage through Counter Crush during every committed dive.

  • Shelly

    Bursts Edgar at point-blank range with concentrated shotgun pellets, denying him time to heal through repeated punches.

  • Chester

    Bursts Edgar at point-blank range because later bell sequences concentrate multiple projectiles where his short punches must connect.

  • Otis

    Silences Edgar on arrival, denying both his attacks and the healing those punches would generate during the duel.

  • Griff

    Bursts Edgar at close range by concentrating every coin, overwhelming his punch healing before it can stabilize the duel.

  • Bull

    Out-tanks Edgar at point-blank range, with Tough Guy reducing his punch damage once Bull drops low.

  • Stu

    Outmaneuvers Edgar with fast Nitro Boost dashes, repeatedly breaking contact before his short punches can restore meaningful health.
