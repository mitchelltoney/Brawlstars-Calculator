Best counters to Bull

  • Nita

    Out-sustains Bull in close fights by cycling healing between herself and her bear whenever either lands damage.

  • Griff

    Bursts through Bull's tank health at close range, and obstacle destruction removes the cover Bull needs to approach.

  • Gale

    Keeps Bull outside effective range with slows and knockback, denying the point-blank damage his tank kit requires.

  • Lumi

    Kites Bull by slowing him with recalled morning stars, repeatedly breaking the close contact his tank pressure requires.
