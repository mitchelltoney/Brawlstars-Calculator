Piper Counters

Best picks against Piper in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Piper

  • Emz

    Punishes Piper whenever cover shortens the lane, stacking spray damage where her distance-scaled shots lose most of their power.

  • Jessie

    Overloads Piper’s slow single-target reload with Scrappy, while Energize enables repeated repairs between her costly sniper shots.

  • Ruffs

    Blocks Piper’s high-damage sniper rounds with Take Cover, forcing her to spend scarce ammo on disposable sandbags.

  • Charlie

    Overloads Piper’s slow single-target reload with Spiders, then cocoons her to remove long-range pressure from the fight.

  • Nani

    Outranges Piper with post-convergence orbs, while Return To Sender punishes one heavy sniper round with reflected damage.

  • Gene

    Pulls Piper out of her preferred long-range lane, forcing close combat where her distance-scaled damage collapses.

  • Nita

    Overloads Piper’s slow single-target reload with her bear, which absorbs shots and forces Piper from open sniper lanes.

    Wins peek trades against Colt by delivering each attack's damage in one bullet while his full volley demands sustained tracking.

  • Crow
