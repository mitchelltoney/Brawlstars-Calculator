Piper Counters

Best picks against Piper in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Piper

  • Emz

    Punishes Piper whenever cover shortens the lane, stacking spray damage where her distance-scaled shots lose most of their power.

  • Jessie

    Overloads Piper’s slow single-target reload with Scrappy, forcing costly sniper shots into a turret Jessie can repeatedly repair.

  • Ruffs

    Blocks Piper’s high-damage sniper rounds with sandbags, forcing her slow reload through disposable cover before he becomes vulnerable.

  • Charlie

    Overloads Piper’s slow single-target reload with seeking spiders, then cocoons her to remove long-range pressure from the fight.

  • Nani

    Outranges Piper with post-convergence orbs and reflects one heavy sniper round, turning her maximum-distance damage against her.

  • Gene

    Pulls Piper out of her preferred long-range lane, forcing close combat where her distance-scaled damage collapses.

  • Nita

    Overloads Piper’s slow single-target reload with her bear, which absorbs shots and forces Piper from open sniper lanes.
