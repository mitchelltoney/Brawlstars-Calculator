Best counters to Bolt

  • Gale

    Disrupts Bolt's speed buildup with slows and knockback before his crowd-control immunity activates.

  • Piper

    Outranges Bolt during his approach, then Auto Aimer knocks him back whenever his crowd-control immunity is inactive.

  • Mandy

    Outranges Bolt while Focused, and Caramelize slows his speed buildup whenever his crowd-control immunity is inactive.

  • Colette

    Chunks Bolt's huge health pool with percentage-based attacks, pressuring him before he reaches full collision speed.

  • Charlie

    Cocoons Bolt mid-charge, halting his approach even while his Super grants crowd-control immunity.

  • Willow

    Mind-controls Bolt after his crowd-control immunity expires, redirecting his approach before his collision reaches its intended target.

Also weak to: Sniper Knockback
