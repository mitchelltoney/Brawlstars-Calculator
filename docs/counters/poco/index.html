Best counters to Poco

  • Gus

    Outranges Poco’s low-damage waves, then absorbs his return fire with Spooky’s shield while landing focused single-target shots.

  • Bo

    Outranges Poco’s low-damage waves and controls his support lane with hidden mines whose stun creates decisive focus-fire windows.

  • Max

    Outranges Poco’s low-damage waves and overwhelms his healing with rapid volleys, reloading faster throughout constant movement.

  • Emz

    Overwhelms Poco’s healing with stacking spray damage, especially when his nearby teammates remain grouped inside the same cloud.

  • Mortis

    Exploits Poco’s low burst by dashing through his wide waves, then drains grouped teammates with a piercing healing Super.

  • Jessie

    Punishes Poco’s grouped support formations with bouncing turret shots, adding sustained damage across teammates sharing his healing.
