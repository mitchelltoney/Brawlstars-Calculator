Poco Counters

Best picks against Poco in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Poco

  • Gus

    Outranges Poco’s low-damage waves, then absorbs his return fire with Spooky’s shield while landing focused single-target shots.

  • Bo

    Outranges Poco’s low-damage waves, while Snare A Bear turns hidden mines into stuns that create focus-fire openings.

  • Max

    Outranges Poco’s low-damage waves and overwhelms his healing with rapid, sustained volleys from four ammo slots.

  • Emz

    Overwhelms Poco’s healing with stacking spray damage, especially when his nearby teammates remain grouped inside the same cloud.

  • Mortis

    Exploits Poco’s low burst by dashing past his wide waves, then drains grouped teammates with a piercing, self-healing Super.

  • Jessie

    Punishes Poco’s grouped formations with bouncing attacks, while Scrappy adds sustained pressure that strains his shared healing.

Also weak to: Thrower

Poco is a strong pick against

  • Lola

    Keeps his team healthy while broad attacks pass through Lola's Ego, striking both and denying the clone's cover.

  • Nita

    Turns Nita's bear into ineffective cover by piercing it, while repeated healing withstands pressure from both opponents.

  • Otis

    Removes Otis's silence from nearby allies and grants temporary debuff immunity through Protective Tunes.

  • Stu

    Offsets Stu's constant poke with healing and lands broad, piercing attacks across the paths of his lateral dashes.
