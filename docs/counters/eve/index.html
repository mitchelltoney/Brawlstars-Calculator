Eve Counters

Best picks against Eve in Brawl Stars ranked · updated July 2026

Best counters to Eve

  • Penny

    Turns Eve’s hatchlings into splash triggers, bursting gold pouches through them to damage her while clearing the swarm.

  • Janet

    Pierces Eve’s hatchlings with focused sonic attacks, clearing the swarm while preserving pressure on Eve herself.

  • Max

    Dodges Eve’s staggered egg volleys with superior speed, then rapid fire clears hatchlings before they establish pressure.

  • Piper

    Outranges Eve’s egg volley and punishes her slow unload, forcing retreats before hatchlings can build pressure.

Also weak to: Sniper

Eve is a strong pick against

  • Gene

    Stays beyond Gene's full-damage range while hatchlings consume his slow-loading shots and intercept his pull.

  • Glowy

    Controls Glowy from beyond beam range while hatchlings strain his single-target damage and apply poison pressure.

  • Najia

    Forces Najia to spend her single ammo on disposable hatchlings, whose defeats cannot activate Poisonous Protector.
