Safe Zone

Safe Zone funnels teams through a long central choke, rewarding sniper control and wall-break while water, unbreakable cover, and direct rail routes keep assassins and base-racers essential.

Strong data

  • Colt

    Straight lanes reward precision bursts; wall-break opens permanent sightlines and massive safe damage.

  • Chuck

    Post routes bypass the center choke, repeatedly crashing through defenders into the safe.

  • Lumi

    Grounded maces bracket the center choke, while returns and stuns punish clustered pushes.

  • Pierce

    The middle choke feeds repeat shots, creating elite defense and sustained long-range lane control.
