The Seven Pillars Of Humanity

Rope fences, water pockets, and broken central cover create midrange funnels where sustain, piercing pressure, and displacement dominate.

Decent data

    Tether healing sustains the zone holder while fear clears clustered enemies from narrow approaches.

  • Finx

    Time Warp wins projectile trades across the exposed center and protects stationary zone control.

  • Pierce

    Piercing fire and slows punish enemies funneling beside water, fences, and central cover.

  • Mina

    Dash mobility reaches side lanes quickly; hurricane displacement ejects defenders from the objective.

  • Najia

    Stacking poison snakes deny cramped approaches and steadily force defenders off capture.
