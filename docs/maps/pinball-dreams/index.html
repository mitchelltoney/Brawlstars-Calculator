Pinball Dreams

An open mid split by side fencing creates short, wall-pocket lanes where anti-tank control and selective wall break decide goals.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

    Builds speed through open mid, then knocks defenders aside or breaks goal walls.

  • Damian

    Goal-side walls amplify his knockback, while mosh-pit jumps trap clustered defenders.

  • Surge

    Short lanes feed early upgrades; jumps punish rushes and create sudden scoring pressure.

  • Chester

    Reliable burst checks tanks in cramped lanes, and a wall-breaking Super exposes the goal.

  • Shade

    Dense wall pockets let him pressure safely through cover and collapse on carriers.
