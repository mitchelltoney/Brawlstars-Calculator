Rustic Arcade

Exposed sniper lanes around a lightly walled center reward ranged control, sustain, and anti-assassin coverage.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Bolt

    Open lanes build his speed safely, then Overdrive dives or escorts the gem carrier.

  • Glowy

    Long beam sustains allies across exposed center and fear dislodges the opposing gem carrier.

  • Lola

    Ego doubles long-range lane pressure while her durability makes her a safe carrier.

  • Ruffs

    Central wall faces enable ricochets, while permanent boosts strengthen both ranged lanes.

  • Pierce

    Exceptional range owns open lanes while anti-assassin tools protect the exposed mid.

Best bans

  • Bolt

    Current Gem Grab leader; open lanes let him reach speed and collapse the carrier.

  • Glowy

    His beam is extremely difficult to break across the exposed central sightlines.

  • Charlie

    Strong map results and 13-counter breadth pair with countdown-flipping Cocoon denial.

  • Stu

    Nineteen-counter breadth and open-lane mobility destabilize slow ranged carrier compositions.

  • Kenji

    Eighteen-counter breadth makes him a premium assassin ban against vulnerable marksman drafts.
