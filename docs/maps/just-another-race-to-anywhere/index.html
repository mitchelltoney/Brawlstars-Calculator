Just Another Race To Anywhere

Two separated zones, central water, and unbreakable walls reward one safe holder plus aggressive wall-crossing or throwing pressure.

Decent data

S tier — best picks

  • Shade

    Attacks through unbreakable zone walls and rapidly invades the enemy-side circle.

  • Doug

    Wall-piercing area healing and damage make the home zone exceptionally difficult to retake.

  • Juju

    Throws over permanent cover while water mobility controls both protected zone approaches.

  • Kenji

    Dash mobility and wall-peeking pressure let him repeatedly contest the enemy-side zone.

  • Trunk

    Durable bush-lane aggression converts close-range wins into sustained enemy-zone pressure.
