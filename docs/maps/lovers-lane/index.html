An exposed bomb-spawn plaza ringed by wall-and-bush pockets rewards marksman pressure, then pickup mobility.

    Long sightlines let her pressure carriers and force stuns before they reach cover.

    Three dashes win bomb races and cross quickly between the surrounding cover pockets.
