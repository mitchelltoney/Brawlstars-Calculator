On A Roll

A wall-and-bush-heavy Gem Grab map where covered side approaches meet the central mine, rewarding durable mobility, burst pressure, and close-mid control.

Decent data

  • Starr Nova

    Walls suit her poke-to-dash rhythm, letting transformed dives steal gems and safely disengage.
