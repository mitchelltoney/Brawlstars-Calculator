Bridge Too Far

Three isolated, water-split sniper lanes make winning duels and converting range into safe damage decisive.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Nani

    Outranges opposing snipers and punishes exposed safe approaches with enormous burst damage.

  • Piper

    Dominates long sightlines, controls narrow lanes, and reloads efficiently while hitting the safe.

  • Mandy

    Focused range wins the longest lane while her Super pierces straight into the safe.

  • Colt

    Shreds the safe from range and opens cover to simplify all three lanes.

  • Bolt

    Wins isolated lane duels, then converts separated fights into overwhelming two-on-one pressure.

Best bans

  • Piper

    CounterPower 20 plus elite sightline control lets her warp nearly every sniper draft.

  • Bolt

    Lane dominance snowballs isolated duels into unavoidable cross-lane two-on-one pressure.

  • Nani

    Wins sniper mirrors, outranges most lanes, and bursts divers before they reach the safe.

  • Belle

    CounterPower 16 and safe first-pick range punish most lane and tank options.

  • Colette

    CounterPower 12 answers 8bit, Chuck, and Melodie while retaining dangerous safe damage.
