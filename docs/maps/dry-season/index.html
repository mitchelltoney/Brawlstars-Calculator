Dry Season

Dry Season is a hybrid long-range Bounty map where open lanes reward marksmen, but central stage walls make assassin and thrower counterpicks decisive.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Mortis

    Dashes secure the blue star and punish snipers and throwers sheltering behind stage walls.

  • Pierce

    Very-long range wins open lanes while jump flexibility survives assassin pressure around stage walls.

  • Najia

    Piercing jars and stacking poison pressure clustered lanes, denying safe retreats behind wall pockets.

  • Edgar

    Wall pockets enable timed dives onto snipers and throwers, converting passive lanes into stars.

  • Brock

    Long rockets control open lanes, while wall breaks expose enemies hiding behind backstage cover.

Best bans

  • Mortis

    Seventeen-counter breadth plus elite assassin pressure makes fragile sniper-and-thrower drafts unsafe.

  • Piper

    Twenty-counter breadth and wall-breaking range erase the map's safest back-wall positions.

  • Edgar

    Current top-five map usage and ten-counter breadth punish unprotected throwers behind walls.

  • Najia

    Top map results and oppressive poison angles justify a strength-first ban despite narrow breadth.

  • Brock

    Lower breadth, but exceptional wall breaking and open-lane pressure reshape the entire map.
