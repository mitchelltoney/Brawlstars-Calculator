Konnakol

Paired central water blocks funnel teams into sniper sightlines and tight crossings, where Bolt-Kit dives and Damian-Colette control dominate.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Bolt

    Open perimeter lanes build speed; shielded dives cross chokepoints before snipers can settle.

  • Kit

    Attaching to Bolt or Damian converts each choke dive into a sustained two-brawler collapse.

  • Damian

    Speaker walls seal water-side passages, while his Super punishes enemies trapped near mid.

  • Colette

    Range controls straight approaches; knockback and percentage damage hard-counter the map's tank core.

  • Starr Nova

    Open-mid chip builds Super, then invulnerable dashes cross water chokes for clean finishes.
