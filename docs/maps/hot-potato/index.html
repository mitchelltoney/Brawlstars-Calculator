Hot Potato

Hot Potato is a wall-heavy Heist race where protected throwers, wall-hopping dives, and Colt's wall-break contest direct safe access.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Berry

    Protected thrower lanes let Berry heal pushes and lob enormous damage onto the safe.

  • Nori

    Wall-hopping hooks bypass central cover, punish throwers, and chain heavy damage through the safe.

  • Colt

    Long side lanes reward his range, while wall-break opens direct safe-damage angles.

  • Colette

    Buffed control checks tank rushes, and repeated Hypercharge supers erase safe health.
