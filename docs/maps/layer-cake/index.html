Layer Cake

Layer Cake is a wall-layered, bushy, draft-sensitive Bounty map where assassins race for the blue star, protected throwers lock chokes, and anti-aggro counters decide last pick.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Edgar

    Layered walls give easy jumps onto throwers, and lifesteal punishes isolated defenders.

  • Mortis

    Fast blue-star access and wall-pocket dashes let him collapse on fragile backliners.

  • Tick

    Mines blanket narrow approaches while walls keep him safe after securing a lead.

  • Mr P

    Double-hit suitcases and porters pressure enemies stacked behind the map's layered walls.

  • Najia

    Long throws and persistent snakes pressure enemies hiding behind Layer Cake's wall bands.

A tier — strong picks

Starr Nova Bolt Meeple Grom Kenji

B tier — solid picks

Penny Griff Squeak Colette Shade

Best bans

  • Edgar

    The dominant wall-hopping assassin here also deletes the throwers most drafts depend upon.

  • Mortis

    Elite blue-star theft plus 17-counter breadth removes too many backline options.

  • Kenji

    Strong wall-pocket pressure and 18-counter breadth make him a premium denial ban.

  • Penny

    Her turret locks narrow retreats while 16-counter breadth punishes grouped defensive comps.

  • Chester

    Fourteen-counter breadth and anti-aggro burst invalidate many assassin-heavy Layer Cake drafts.
