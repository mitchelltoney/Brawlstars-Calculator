Center Stage

Dense central grass, fenced side lanes, and a narrow goal create a tank-heavy vision-and-wall-break draft where anti-tank damage is mandatory.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Damian

    Overwhelms low-DPS mids through bush lanes and converts lane pressure into fast goals.

  • Griff

    Shreds tank pushes while his wall break strips cover from the three-tile goal.

  • Jacky

    Bush-ring ambushes let her close instantly, pierce clustered defenders, and force goals.

  • Rosa

    Dense central grass sustains her healing, approach, and durable ball-carrying pressure.

  • Otis

    Silence shuts down bush-lane tanks and assassins before they can force the goal.

Best bans

  • Frank

    Eighteen-counter breadth plus bush-lane tank pressure can collapse an entire draft.

  • Chester

    Fourteen-counter breadth and burst punish nearly every tank or carrier entering bushes.

  • Jacky

    Top map win rates and ten-counter breadth make bush ambushes draft-warping.

  • Otis

  • Damian

    Elite current win rates make his bush-lane pressure dangerous despite narrower counter breadth.
