Shooting Star

Shooting Star is a very open Bounty map where long-range lane control dominates, while center cover enables artillery and late-pick dives.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Bolt

    Open loops preserve his speed, letting him dive fragile snipers and escape with stars.

  • Piper

    Her newly buffed long-range shots dominate open lanes while Super provides emergency escape.

  • Najia

    Strong recent win-rate results combine lane pressure with summons that drain enemy sniper ammo.

  • Pierce

    Extreme range and reliable finishing pressure secure kills before high-bounty targets can retreat.

  • Nani

    Her converging shots outrange rival snipers while Peep threatens passive backline star holders.

Best bans

  • Piper

    Map-defining range plus 20-brawler counter coverage makes her the premium broad ban.

  • Mortis

    His 17-brawler counter coverage and strong current results punish sniper-artillery drafts lacking hard control.

  • Bolt

    Even after July nerfs, open lanes produce map-leading results and relentless backline dives.

  • Nani

    She wins long-range mirrors, counters Piper, and forces unsafe movement with Peep.

  • Najia

    Consistently elite map stats and ammo-taxing summons make passive star leads hard to protect.
