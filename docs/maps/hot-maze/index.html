Hot Maze

A compact, asymmetric maze of few entrances where wall pressure, ricochets, and mobile collapses decide mystery-box control before carryables open the center.

Early estimates

S tier — best picks

    Rolling mobility claims maze entrances quickly, while his Hypercharge can erase obstructing walls.

  • Starr Nova

    Repeated dashes cross cramped entrances, and transformed sustain preserves valuable Loaded power-ups.

    Teammate attachment and stuns turn fortified maze fights into safe two-brawler collapses.

  • Damian

    Bouncing speakers punish narrow passages, giving his duo control over central mystery boxes.

  • Ollie

    Top ordinary-Duo survivor; tankiness anchors the open center and survives Loaded item chaos.
