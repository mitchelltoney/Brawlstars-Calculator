Gem Fort

A bush-heavy, wall-ringed mine rewards anti-aggro carriers, vision, and explosive dives through four narrow entrances.

Decent data

S tier — best picks

  • Damian

    Speaker walls trap clustered defenders around the mine and enable decisive gem-swinging team wipes.

  • Bolt

    Speed and self-healing overwhelm bush lanes, then rotate quickly around the fortified mine.

  • Crow

    Wide poison spread safely scouts bush lanes while suppressing healing around the gem carrier.

  • Otis

    Reliable anti-aggro silence locks narrow entrances and stabilizes a first-pick gem carrier.

  • Starr Nova

    Fast projectiles hold a bush entrance; her dash converts lane wins into mine pressure.

A tier — strong picks

Chester Tara Jessie Bo Colette

B tier — solid picks

Mortis Edgar Nita Poco Surge

Best bans

  • Damian

    Map-leading pressure and mine traps can instantly reverse the gem count.

  • Mortis

    Seventeen-counter breadth plus wall-fed dives make late-countdown gem steals too dangerous.

  • Chester

    Fourteen-counter breadth and anti-tank burst punish nearly every safe carrier draft.

  • Colette

    Twelve-counter breadth, tank deletion, and Hypercharge steals punish this cramped fort.

  • Edgar

    Ten-counter breadth plus wall-fed dives punish the fragile control picks this map encourages.
