Pit Stop

Dense bushes and indestructible safe walls create a close-range, wall-bypassing base race with limited reliable defense.

Strong data

  • Mico

    Wall-hopping bypasses the fortified approach, making repeated safe dives brutally hard to defend.

  • Bibi

    Speed and knockback dominate cramped lanes, then bubble pressure accelerates the base race.

  • Shade

    Wall-piercing attacks and phasing punish defenders clustered behind the safe's fortifications.

  • Cordelius

    Shadow Realm removes key defenders while short lanes keep pressure immediately threatening.
