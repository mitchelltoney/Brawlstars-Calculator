Chivalry

Chivalry is an exposed mid-control map where long-range pressure, support-backed pinches, and protected side advances trap teams in a narrow retreat channel.

Strong data

S tier — best picks

  • Pierce

    Auto-targeted shell shots pierce retreat cover, while his defensive gadget deters side-lane dives.

  • Piper

    Buffed range damage dominates exposed mid, while wallbreak removes the last safe retreat cover.

  • Nani

    Long center sightlines maximize burst, and Peep forces snipers out of rear cover.

  • Bolt

    Protected side corridors let him build speed, then collapse on pinned marksmen.

  • Najia

    Stacking poison denies healing between trades and snakes clog the single retreat channel.

Best bans

  • Piper

    CounterPower 20 plus her July buffs make open-mid drafts revolve around answering her.

  • Belle

    CounterPower 16 and bouncing pressure punish teams forced to regroup in the retreat lane.

  • Pierce

    The safest current marksman converts one hit into overwhelming pressure and survives counter-dives.

  • Nani

    She hard-checks Piper and deletes slow marksmen across Chivalry's longest central sightlines.

  • Bolt

    His map-leading results and side-corridor pinch threat outweigh his narrower counter breadth.
