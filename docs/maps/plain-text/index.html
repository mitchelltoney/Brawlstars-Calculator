Curved wall blockades and mandatory bush lanes create a close-range Heist race where wall-jumpers, tanks, and rebound damage beat pure snipers.

S tier — best picks

  • Nori

    Wall-jumping bypasses Plain Text's curved blockades, and dash tech supplies exceptional safe DPS.

  • Bull

    Bush-fed lanes let him close safely, then unload huge burst onto the safe.

    Curved walls multiply Super bounces while speed and knockback secure close safe access.

  • Mico

    Walls enable repeated dives; his safe-bursting jump pressure forces dedicated defensive answers.

  • Nita

    Short bush lanes charge Bruce quickly, sustaining lane pressure and unattended safe damage.
